# Reactome Disease Catalogue

## Overview

Catalogue of diseases in the [Reactome](https://reactome.org/) pathway database,
cross-referenced with dismech entries. Reactome curates disease-associated
pathway variants, providing mechanistic context for how mutations perturb
normal biological processes.

- **Not yet in dismech**: 660
