Used for adult natural history and treatment indications.

Key snippets:

- "Secundum atrial septal defect (ASDII) is a common congenital heart defect

- "If the defect remains untreated, however, the rates of exercise intolerance,

  arterial hypertension (PAH) increase with patient age, and life expectancy is
  reduced."
- "Transcatheter and surgical techniques both provide valid options for ASDII
  closure, the former being the preferred method."
