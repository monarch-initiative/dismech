# Citations for Bardet-Biedl Syndrome Deep Research

This sidecar lists the primary literature used to build
`Bardet-Biedl_Syndrome-deep-research-openai.md` and the final disorder YAML.

1. PMID:22109794. Tunisian prevalence study used for epidemiology framing and
   the quoted European prevalence estimate.
2. PMID:30614526. Molecularly confirmed prenatal cohort used for disease
   framing, genetic heterogeneity, and frequent prenatal phenotypes.
3. PMID:35886001. German ophthalmic cohort used for retinal dystrophy
   invariance, progressive visual loss, and BBS10/BBS1 genotype prevalence.
4. PMID:28143435. Italian cohort used for BBS1/BBS10 prevalence and BBS10
   severity notes.
5. PMID:22500027. Mechanistic BBSome assembly study used for the proximal
   assembly-defect node.
6. PMID:20080638. Complementary BBS-chaperonin/BBSome assembly study used for
   the same proximal mechanism.
7. PMID:36699005. Mouse neuronal cilia study used for the ciliary cargo
   trafficking defect node.
8. PMID:19150989. Mouse leptin-receptor trafficking study used for the
   hypothalamic leptin-signaling node.
9. PMID:21209035. Human metabolic study used for hyperleptinemia and clinical
   leptin resistance.
10. PMID:23776152. Human hyperphagia study used for the hyperphagia phenotype.
11. PMID:15539463. Bbs2-null mouse retinopathy study used for the
    photoreceptor transport defect node.
12. PMID:18381349. Zebrafish limb-patterning study used for the
    hedgehog-dependent polydactyly mechanism.
13. PMID:20876674. French renal cohort used for inheritance, renal
    pathophysiology, and renal phenotype frequency.
14. PMID:32700463. Pediatric registry used for early obesity trajectory.
15. PMID:36356613. Phase 3 setmelanotide trial used for obesity treatment
    evidence.
16. PMID:22358239. Retinal-function cohort used for progressive visual loss.
17. PMID:41219488. Large Turkish cohort used for recent phenotype frequencies
    and modern genetic heterogeneity.
18. PMID:37031301. Chinese cohort used to avoid overstating renal invariance.
19. PMID:27245600. Kidney transplantation series used for the treatment entry in
    the ESRD subset.
