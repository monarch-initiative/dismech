Its pathogenesis involves complex interactions across numerous cellular and molecular pathways, highlighting its unique impact on physiological processes. As a result, personalized therapeutic interventions, such as immune response modulation, the restoration of cellular metabolism, and therapies targeting mitochondrial function, are emerging as potential strategies to address the diverse clinical manifestations of long COVID.     > However, significant challenges remain. The wide range of manifestations, including respiratory, neurological, and metabolic, suggests multiple distinct pathophysiological pathways. This diversity complicates data interpretation, as integrating findings across heterogeneous populations may dilute individual-specific signals or obscure relevant associations.
