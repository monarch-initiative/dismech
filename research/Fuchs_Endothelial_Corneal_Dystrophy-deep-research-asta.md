Corneal endothelial cells (CECs) in FECD patients demonstrate enhanced vulnerability to apoptosis triggered by oxidative stress compared with endothelial cells from healthy controls [11,12]. This susceptibility stems from multiple factors, including accumulated oxidative damage to DNA (particularly affecting mitochondrial DNA) and compromised antioxidant defense systems [11]. Cell death in FECD is further promoted by chronic activation of the unfolded protein response pathway [13][14][15].
