Schmid type metaphyseal chondrodysplasia (SMCD) is a rare skeletal dysplasia, 
characterized by short stature, short limbs, bowing of the legs, and 
radiographic features of metaphyseal irregularities with fraying and splaying, 
more severe at the knee. It is caused by mutations of the COL10A1 gene. The 
authors present an Indian patient with a novel COL10A1 gene mutation.
