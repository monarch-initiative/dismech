Two studies published in this issue of JEM, by Lepelley et al. 
(https://doi.org/10.1084/jem.20200600) and Deng et al. 
(https://doi.org/10.1084/jem.20201045), and two additional manuscripts by Mukai 
et al. (https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.05.20.107664 Preprint v1) and Steiner et 
al. (https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.07.09.194399 Preprint v1) demonstrate that 
COPA syndrome-associated high interferon titers are linked to mutations in COPA 
preventing STING's retrieval from the Golgi back to the ER and thereby causing 
chronic immune activation.
