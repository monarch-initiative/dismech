**MYGENESET:FRASOR_RESPONSE_TO_ESTRADIOL_UP** — FRASOR_RESPONSE_TO_ESTRADIOL_UP (msigdb, C2:CGP)

## Description

MSigDB C2:CGP gene set of genes upregulated in MCF-7 breast cancer cells following treatment with 17β-estradiol (E2; Frasor et al. 2004, PMID:14973112). This is a chemical/hormone perturbation UP signature: member genes are induced specifically by the sex steroid 17β-estradiol acting through nuclear estrogen receptor alpha (ERα) in an ER-positive luminal breast cancer cell line. The 35-member set captures the canonical estradiol transcriptional response in breast cancer cells, including cell-cycle promoting, growth-factor signalling, and ER co-regulatory genes, and is widely used as an in vitro estrogen response reference in breast cancer transcriptomics.
