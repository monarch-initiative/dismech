# Pregnancy in women with Noonan syndrome: report of two cases
**Authors:** A J Cullimore et al.
**Journal:** Obstet Gynecol (1999)
**DOI:** [10.1016/S0029-7844(98)00413-X](https://doi.org/10.1016/S0029-7844(98)00413-X)

## Content

Background: Noonan syndrome is similar phenotypically to Turner syndrome, accounting for one in 1000-2500 live births. Two thirds of patients have cardiac anomalies, half with pulmonary stenosis.
