# ORPHA:100924  Porphyria due to ALA dehydratase deficiency

**ORPHA:100924** — Porphyria due to ALA dehydratase deficiency (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- ALAD porphyria
- Porphyria due to ALAD deficiency
- Porphyria due to delta-aminolevulinate dehydratase deficiency
- Porphyria of Doss

## Definition

A rare acute hepatic porphyria characterized by neurovisceral attacks without skin symptoms.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Childhood
