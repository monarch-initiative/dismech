Barata IA(1).

The diagnosis and management of pediatric cardiac emergencies can be challenging 
and complicated. Early presentations are usually the result of ductal-dependent 
lesions and appear with cyanosis and shock. Later presentations are the result 
of volume overload or pump failure and present with signs of congestive heart 
failure. Acquired diseases also present as congestive heart failure or 
arrhythmias.
