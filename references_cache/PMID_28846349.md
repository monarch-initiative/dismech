Atopic dermatitis (AD), which is a specific form of eczema, is the most common 
chronic inflammatory skin disease. This chronic disorder associated with 
pruritus usually starts in infancy and presents with dry skin, eczematous 
lesions and lichenification. It is believed that AD is associated with other IgE 
associated disorders like allergic rhinitis, asthma, and food allergies. AD has 
significant morbidity and it appears that the prevalence of the disorder has 
been increasing over the past few decades.
