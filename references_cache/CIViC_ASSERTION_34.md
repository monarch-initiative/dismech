## Summary

ALK fusion positive NSCLC is sensitive to alectinib

## Description

ALK fusions are found in approximately 5% of NSCLC patients. These fusions act as driver mutations through ligand-independent dimerization of ALK and constitutive downstream pathway activation. Alectinib in ALK-positive metastatic NSCLC has shown superior performance compared to first-line crizotinib in two phase 3 trials (civic.eid:8657 and civic.eid:7284). The VENTANA ALK (D5F3) CDx assay is FDA approved as companion diagnostic.
