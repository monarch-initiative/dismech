The α-thalassemias.

Piel FB, Weatherall DJ.

More than 100 varieties of α-thalassemia have been identified. Their geographic 
distribution and the challenges associated with screening, diagnosis, and 
management suggest that α-thalassemias should have a higher priority on global 
public health agendas.
