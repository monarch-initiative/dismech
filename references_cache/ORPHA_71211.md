# ORPHA:71211  Neuromyelitis optica spectrum disorder

**ORPHA:71211** — Neuromyelitis optica spectrum disorder (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Devic disease
- NMOSD

## Definition

A rare inflammatory disease of the central nervous system characterized mainly by attacks of uni- or bilateral optic neuritis (ON) and acute myelitis.
