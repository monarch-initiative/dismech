# Hematuria as presenting sign in Wissler-Fanconi syndrome.
**Authors:** von Kemp K, Dehaen F, Huybrechts M, Verbruggen L
**Journal:** J Rheumatol (1987)

## Content

1. J Rheumatol. 1987 Feb;14(1):145-6.

Hematuria as presenting sign in Wissler-Fanconi syndrome.

von Kemp K, Dehaen F, Huybrechts M, Verbruggen L.

We describe a case of persistent microscopic hematuria as initial finding in 
incomplete Still's disease or Wissler-Fanconi syndrome. Renal biopsy findings 
were compatible with intravascular coagulation. Wissler-Fanconi syndrome and the 
associated renal abnormalities are briefly reviewed.
