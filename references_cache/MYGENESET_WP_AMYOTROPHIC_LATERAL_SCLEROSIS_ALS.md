**MYGENESET:WP_AMYOTROPHIC_LATERAL_SCLEROSIS_ALS** — WP_AMYOTROPHIC_LATERAL_SCLEROSIS_ALS (msigdb, C2:CP:WIKIPATHWAYS)

## Description

WikiPathways amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set, collection C2:CP:WIKIPATHWAYS). This is a disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. ALS is a fatal progressive neurodegenerative disease characterised by selective degeneration of upper and lower motor neurons, leading to paralysis and respiratory failure. At 38 member genes this set is more compact than the legacy KEGG ALS pathway; it captures the core mechanistic arms of ALS pathobiology as represented in WikiPathways: SOD1-mediated oxidative stress, glutamate excitotoxicity through ionotropic receptors (AMPA/NMDA), mitochondrial dysfunction, and downstream apoptotic execution of motor neurons. It is a distinct gene set from KEGG_AMYOTROPHIC_LATERAL_SCLEROSIS_ALS (different source, different membership) and is curated independently.
