(1)Victorian Clinical Genetics Service, Royal Children's Hospital, Parkville,

The combination of spondyloepiphyseal dysplasia, humoral immune deficiency, 
growth retardation, intellectual deficit and characteristic facial dysmorphism 
has recently been delineated as a discrete disorder thus far only reported in 
males. This report describes a fifth individual with co-existent 
hypogonadotrophic hypogonadism, thereby expanding the phenotype and possibly 
offering insight into the genetic aetiology of this condition.
