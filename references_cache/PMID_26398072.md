The Asthma-COPD Overlap Syndrome.

Postma DS, Rabe KF.

Although in textbooks asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) 
are viewed as distinct disorders, there is increasing awareness that many 
patients have features of both. This article reviews the asthma-COPD overlap 
syndrome.
