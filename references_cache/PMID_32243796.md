Ervebo is the first licensed vaccine for prevention of Ebola virus disease. The 
vaccine, originally developed by the Public Health Agency of Canada, is 
delivered in a single 1 mL dose and has been delivered to >200,000 people in an 
ongoing 2018-2020 outbreak of disease. To view this Bench to Bedside, open or
