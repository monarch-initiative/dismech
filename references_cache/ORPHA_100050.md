# ORPHA:100050  Hereditary angioedema type 1

**ORPHA:100050** — Hereditary angioedema type 1 (Etiological subtype, Subtype of disorder)

## Synonyms

- HAE 1
- HAE-I
- Hereditary angioneurotic edema type 1

## Definition

A form of hereditary angioedema characterized by acute edema in subcutaneous tissues, viscera and/or the upper airway.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: All ages
