# ORPHA:199348  Thiamine-responsive encephalopathy

**ORPHA:199348** — Thiamine-responsive encephalopathy (Disease, Disorder)

## Definition

Thiamine-responsive encephalopathy is a Wernicke-like encephalopathy characterized by seizures responsive to high doses of thiamine.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
