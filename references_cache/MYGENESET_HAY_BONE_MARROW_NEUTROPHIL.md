# MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_NEUTROPHIL  HAY_BONE_MARROW_NEUTROPHIL

**MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_NEUTROPHIL** — HAY_BONE_MARROW_NEUTROPHIL (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of neutrophils (granulocytes) from human bone marrow (Hay et al.). Neutrophils are the most abundant circulating leukocytes and the first-responder cells of innate immunity, specialised for phagocytosis of microorganisms, degranulation of preformed cytotoxic granules (azurophil, specific, and gelatinase granules), and generation of a respiratory burst via NADPH oxidase.
