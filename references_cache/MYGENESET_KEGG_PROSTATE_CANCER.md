# MYGENESET:KEGG_PROSTATE_CANCER  KEGG_PROSTATE_CANCER

**MYGENESET:KEGG_PROSTATE_CANCER** — KEGG_PROSTATE_CANCER (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy prostate cancer pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a cancer disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes are dominated by androgen-receptor (AR) signaling components (AR, KLK3/PSA, FKBP5, SRD5A1/2), PI3K/AKT effectors (PIK3CA, PTEN, AKT1), RAS/MAPK pathway members (KRAS, BRAF, MAP2K1, MAPK1/3), cell-cycle regulators acting through the RB1/TP53 axis (RB1, E2F1, CDKN1A/CDKN2A), and apoptosis modulators (TP53, BCL2, CASP3), reflecting the major mechanistic arms of the KEGG prostate cancer pathway: androgen-driven transcription, PI3K survival signaling, MAPK-mediated proliferation, cell-cycle dysregulation, and apoptosis evasion.
