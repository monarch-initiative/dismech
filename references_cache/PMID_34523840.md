# [A young woman with severe dehydration].
**Authors:** Haverkamp GLG, Castelijn DAR, Bomers MK

A 28-year-old female presented with severe dehydration due to acute diarrhea and 
vomiting, which she developed after returning from a vacation in Togo. Her stool 
was watery, with flecks of mucous, also referred to as "rice-water" stool, which 
is typical for cholera and can amount to 1000 cc/hour. The infection was 
confirmedby stool culture.
