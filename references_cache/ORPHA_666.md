# ORPHA:666  Osteogenesis imperfecta

**ORPHA:666** — Osteogenesis imperfecta (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Brittle bone disease
- Glass bone disease
- Lobstein disease
- OI

## Definition

A rare, genetic, primary bone dysplasias characterized by increased bone fragility, low bone mass, and susceptibility to bone fractures. The clinical severity is heterogeneous.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant
- Autosomal recessive
- X-linked recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: All ages
