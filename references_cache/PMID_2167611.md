# Autosomal recessive ectodermal dysplasia, cleft lip/palate, mental retardation, and syndactyly: the Zlotogora-Ogur syndrome.
**Authors:** Rodini ES, Richieri-Costa A
**Journal:** Am J Med Genet (1990)
**DOI:** [10.1002/ajmg.1320360420](https://doi.org/10.1002/ajmg.1320360420)

## Content

1. Am J Med Genet. 1990 Aug;36(4):473-6. doi: 10.1002/ajmg.1320360420.

Autosomal recessive ectodermal dysplasia, cleft lip/palate, mental retardation, 
and syndactyly: the Zlotogora-Ogur syndrome.

Rodini ES(1), Richieri-Costa A.

Author information:
(1)Laboratório de Genética Humana, Hospital de Pesquisa e Reabilitação de Lesões 
Lábio-Palatais, Bauru, Brazil.

We report on 3 Brazilian brothers born to normal consanguineous parents (F = 
1/16) and presenting ectodermal dysplasia, cleft lip/palate, mental retardation, 
syndactyly of fingers 2-3, accessory nipples, and ear anomalies. The 
similarities of these 3 brothers to previously reported cases and the pattern of 
inheritance are discussed.
