# ORPHA:3318  Essential thrombocythemia

**ORPHA:3318** — Essential thrombocythemia (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- ET
- Essential thrombocytosis

## Definition

Essential thrombocythemia (ET) is a myeloproliferative neoplasm (MPN) characterized by a sustained elevation of platelet number (> 450 x 109/L) with a tendency for thrombosis and hemorrhage.

## Inheritance

- Multigenic/multifactorial
- Not applicable

## Natural history

- Age of onset: All ages
