# MYGENESET:DESCARTES_FETAL_CEREBRUM_EXCITATORY_NEURONS  DESCARTES_FETAL_CEREBRUM_EXCITATORY_NEURONS

**MYGENESET:DESCARTES_FETAL_CEREBRUM_EXCITATORY_NEURONS** — DESCARTES_FETAL_CEREBRUM_EXCITATORY_NEURONS (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of excitatory (glutamatergic) neurons from human fetal cerebrum (Descartes atlas). These cells are of neural lineage, not hematopoietic, and produce and release glutamate as their primary neurotransmitter. Excitatory neurons are the most abundant neuron class in the cerebral cortex and are characterized by glutamate-mediated chemical synaptic transmission, formation of axons and dendrites, and action-potential-driven neurotransmitter secretion at synaptic terminals.
