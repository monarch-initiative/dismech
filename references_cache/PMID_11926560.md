Role of spleen enlargement in cirrhosis with portal hypertension.

Author information:
(1)Department of Clinical and Experimental Medicine, University of Padua, Italy. 
massimo.bolognesi@unipd.it

The possible relationships between splenomegaly and portal hypertension have 
been analysed in patients with cirrhosis. In this condition, splenomegaly is not 
only caused by portal congestion, but it is mainly due to tissue hyperplasia and 
fibrosis. The increase in spleen size is followed by an increase in splenic 
blood flow, which participates in portal hypertension actively congesting the 
portal system.
