# MYGENESET:WANG_RESPONSE_TO_GSK3_INHIBITOR_SB216763_UP  WANG_RESPONSE_TO_GSK3_INHIBITOR_SB216763_UP

**MYGENESET:WANG_RESPONSE_TO_GSK3_INHIBITOR_SB216763_UP** — WANG_RESPONSE_TO_GSK3_INHIBITOR_SB216763_UP (msigdb, C2:CGP)

## Description

MSigDB C2:CGP chemical-perturbation (UP) signature of genes upregulated in RS4;11 cells (MLL-rearranged B-precursor acute leukemia) following treatment with SB216763, a selective small-molecule inhibitor of GSK3B (glycogen synthase kinase 3 beta). Source: Wang et al. 2010 (PMID:20541704), Cancer Cell 17:597-608. GSK-3 maintains the MLL leukemia stem cell transcriptional program via CREB/TORC/CBP-MEIS1-HOX; its inhibition disrupts this oncogenic axis and concomitantly stabilises beta-catenin, engaging canonical Wnt signalling. The 392-gene UP signature therefore reflects both de-repression of differentiation programmes and induction of Wnt/beta-catenin transcriptional targets in MLL-transformed cells.
