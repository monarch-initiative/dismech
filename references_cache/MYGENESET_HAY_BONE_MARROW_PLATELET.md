# MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_PLATELET  HAY_BONE_MARROW_PLATELET

**MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_PLATELET** — HAY_BONE_MARROW_PLATELET (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of platelets from human bone marrow (Hay et al., MSigDB C8). Platelets are small, anucleate cell fragments derived from megakaryocytes in the bone marrow via thrombopoiesis. They are the primary cellular effectors of hemostasis and thrombosis, becoming activated upon vascular injury to adhere to the subendothelial matrix, aggregate, and release granule contents that propagate the hemostatic response and promote fibrin clot formation.
