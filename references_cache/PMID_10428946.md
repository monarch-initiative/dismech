We have evaluated for the first time in vitro antibiotic susceptibilities of
four human strains of Bartonella bacilliformis, the agent of Carrion's disease.
Our results show that B. bacilliformis, like other Bartonella species, is highly
susceptible to antibiotics, including most beta-lactams, aminoglycosides,
chloramphenicol, rifampin, macrolides, tetracyclines, cotrimoxazole, and
fluoroquinolones.
