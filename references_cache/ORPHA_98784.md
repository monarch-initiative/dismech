# ORPHA:98784  Sleep-related hypermotor epilepsy

**ORPHA:98784** — Sleep-related hypermotor epilepsy (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- ADNFLE
- Autosomal dominant nocturnal frontal lobe epilepsy
- Autosomal dominant sleep-related hypermotor epilepsy
- SHE

## Definition

A rare seizure disorder characterized by intermittent dystonia and/or choreoathetoid movements that occur during sleep. The clusters of nocturnal motor seizures are often stereotyped and brief.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Adult
- Age of onset: Childhood
