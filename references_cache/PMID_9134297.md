# Fibrochondrogenesis: clinical and radiological features.
**Authors:** al-Gazali LI, Bakalinova D, Bakir M, Dawodu A
**Journal:** Clin Dysmorphol (1997)

## Content

1. Clin Dysmorphol. 1997 Apr;6(2):157-63.

Fibrochondrogenesis: clinical and radiological features.

al-Gazali LI(1), Bakalinova D, Bakir M, Dawodu A.

Author information:
(1)Department of Paediatrics, Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences, United 
Arab Emirates University, Al Ain, United Arab Emirates. paed@medic.uaeu.ac.ae

We report an Omani child with fibrochondrogenesis. This neonatally lethal bone 
dysplasia is characterized by a distinctive facial appearance and specific 
radiological changes. Only seven cases have been reported previously. The 
clinical and radiological features together with the differential diagnosis is 
discussed.
