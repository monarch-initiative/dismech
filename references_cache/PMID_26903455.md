Genetic testing is aiding rapid diagnosis of Marfan syndrome as a basis for 
management of eye, heart and skeletal disease. The affected patient's mutation 
can be used as a basis for prenatal or postnatal diagnosis of offspring. 
Preimplantation genetic diagnosis, the technique of choice, can ensure an 
unaffected pregnancy.
