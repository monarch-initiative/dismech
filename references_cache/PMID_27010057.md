The purpose of this overview is to: 1.. Describe the clinical characteristics of 
tubulinopathies. 2.. Review the genetic causes of tubulinopathies. 3.. Review 
the differential diagnosis of tubulinopathies. 4.. Provide an evaluation 
strategy to identify the genetic cause of a tubulinopathy in a proband (when 
possible). 5.. Review general medical management of tubulinopathies. 6.. Inform 
genetic counseling of family members of an individual with a tubulinopathy.
