# MYGENESET:KEGG_SYSTEMIC_LUPUS_ERYTHEMATOSUS  KEGG_SYSTEMIC_LUPUS_ERYTHEMATOSUS

**MYGENESET:KEGG_SYSTEMIC_LUPUS_ERYTHEMATOSUS** — KEGG_SYSTEMIC_LUPUS_ERYTHEMATOSUS (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy systemic lupus erythematosus pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a disease pathway gene set representing the immunopathological mechanisms of systemic lupus erythematosus, a systemic autoimmune disease. Member genes are heavily enriched for nucleosome and histone components (autoantigens targeted by SLE-defining anti-dsDNA and anti-histone autoantibodies), complement pathway components (classical pathway activation by immune complexes), Fc-gamma receptors (immune complex clearance and effector signaling), MHC class II molecules (autoantigen presentation), and B/T cell signaling components, reflecting the major mechanistic arms of the KEGG SLE pathway: autoantigen exposure, immune complex formation, complement-mediated inflammation, and adaptive immune dysregulation.
