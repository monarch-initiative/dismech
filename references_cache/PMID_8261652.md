Dacryocystitis associated with osteopoikilosis.

Günal I(1), Seber S, Başaran N, Artan S, Günal K, Göktürk E.

Author information:
(1)Department of Orthopedics and Traumatology, Anadolu University, Medical 
Faculty, Eskisehir, Turkey.

We report five members of a family with dacryocystitis associated with 
osteopoikilosis. The inheritance is autosomal dominant. Review of the literature 
revealed no other report of this kind of association. Osteopoikilosis must not 
be considered as a coincidental radiographic finding but as part of a systemic 
disorder.
