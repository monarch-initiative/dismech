Bladder diverticula and Menkes' syndrome.

Harcke HT Jr, Capitanio MA, Grover WD, Valdes-Dapena M.

Multiple unusual diverticula of the bladder were observed in 3 of 4 children 
with Menkes' syndrome. This abnormality of the bladder in children with the kiky 
hair syndrome has only recently been recognized. The diverticula are best 
visualized on cystographic studies. The clinical manifestation which led to 
roentgen evaluation of the urinary tract in the 3 children was urinary tract 
infection or urine retention. Though the etiology of these diverticula is 
unknown, evidence is presented to suggest that they are acquired.
