# MYGENESET:KEGG_COLORECTAL_CANCER  KEGG_COLORECTAL_CANCER

**MYGENESET:KEGG_COLORECTAL_CANCER** — KEGG_COLORECTAL_CANCER (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy colorectal cancer pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a cancer disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes are heavily enriched for Wnt/beta-catenin signaling components (APC, CTNNB1, FZD family, TCF/LEF), RAS/MAPK pathway members (KRAS, BRAF, MAP2K1, MAPK1/3), PI3K/AKT effectors (PIK3CA, PTEN, AKT1), cyclin-dependent kinase regulators driving proliferation, and apoptosis modulators (TP53, CASP3, BCL2-family), reflecting the major mechanistic arms of the KEGG colorectal cancer pathway: Wnt-driven initiation, MAPK-mediated proliferation, PI3K survival signaling, cell migration, and apoptosis dysregulation.
