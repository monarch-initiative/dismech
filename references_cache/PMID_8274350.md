# Understanding Ehlers-Danlos syndrome.
**Authors:** Hagen K

## Content

1. Dermatol Nurs. 1993 Dec;5(6):431-4.

Understanding Ehlers-Danlos syndrome.

Hagen K.

Ehlers-Danlos syndrome is an inherited condition in which the body produces 
defective collagen. Manifestations are highly variable, and include numerous 
skin abnormalities. Nurses play an important role in helping patients and their 
families live with this unusual disorder.
