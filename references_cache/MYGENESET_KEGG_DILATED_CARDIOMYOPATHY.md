# MYGENESET:KEGG_DILATED_CARDIOMYOPATHY  KEGG_DILATED_CARDIOMYOPATHY

**MYGENESET:KEGG_DILATED_CARDIOMYOPATHY** — KEGG_DILATED_CARDIOMYOPATHY (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy dilated cardiomyopathy pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes are dominated by sarcomere and contractile apparatus proteins (actin, myosin heavy and light chains, troponins, tropomyosin, titin), the dystrophin-glycoprotein complex (dystrophin/DMD, sarcoglycans, dystrobrevin), cytoskeletal and ECM linkage components, and adrenergic/calcium-handling regulators whose loss-of- function or structural disruption causes ventricular dilation and systolic dysfunction. As a KEGG legacy pathway it captures established mechanistic arms and reflects the major gene families whose mutations underlie familial dilated cardiomyopathy.
