# ORPHA:752  46,XY difference of sex development due to 17-beta-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase 3 deficiency

**ORPHA:752** — 46,XY difference of sex development due to 17-beta-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase 3 deficiency (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- 17-beta-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase 3 deficiency
- 17-ketoreductase deficiency
- 17-ketosteroidreductase deficiency
- 46,XY disorder of sex development due to 17-beta-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase 3 deficiency

## Definition

A rare difference of sex development characterized by 17-beta hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase 3 deficiency that affects individuals with a 46,XY karyotype leading to underandrogenization of the genitalia.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Neonatal
