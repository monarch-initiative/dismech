[Follicular thyroid carcinoma metastatic to the lung and bone and cutaneous: one 
case report].

[Article in Chinese]

Tian ZB, Jin RS, Jin XM.

Follicular thyroid carcinoma usually metastasizes through a hematogenous route, 
the most frequent sites of distant metastases are bone and lung, cutaneous 
metastasis is exceptionally rare. In this paper, we report a patient with lung, 
bone and subcutaneous metastasis of well-differentiated forms of follicular 
thyroid carcinoma, 19 years after resection of thyroid tumor.
