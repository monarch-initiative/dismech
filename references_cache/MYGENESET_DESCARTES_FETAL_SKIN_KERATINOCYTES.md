# MYGENESET:DESCARTES_FETAL_SKIN_KERATINOCYTES  DESCARTES_FETAL_SKIN_KERATINOCYTES

**MYGENESET:DESCARTES_FETAL_SKIN_KERATINOCYTES** — DESCARTES_FETAL_SKIN_KERATINOCYTES (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of keratinocytes from human fetal skin (Descartes atlas). Keratinocytes are the predominant cell type of the epidermis; they undergo a tightly orchestrated program of terminal differentiation as they migrate from the basal layer to the stratum corneum, progressively accumulating keratin intermediate filaments and ultimately assembling the cornified envelope that forms the physical skin barrier. This fetal signature captures early-stage keratinocyte identity and differentiation in developing skin.
