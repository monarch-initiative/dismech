The purpose of this overview is to: 1.. Describe the clinical characteristics of 
CMT hereditary neuropathy; 2.. Review the causes of CMT hereditary neuropathy; 
3.. Provide an evaluation strategy to identify the cause of CMT hereditary 
neuropathy in a proband (when possible); 4.. Review management of CMT hereditary 
neuropathy; 5.. Inform genetic counseling of family members of an individual 
with CMT hereditary neuropathy.
