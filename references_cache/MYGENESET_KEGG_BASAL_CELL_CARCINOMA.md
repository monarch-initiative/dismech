# MYGENESET:KEGG_BASAL_CELL_CARCINOMA  KEGG_BASAL_CELL_CARCINOMA

**MYGENESET:KEGG_BASAL_CELL_CARCINOMA** — KEGG_BASAL_CELL_CARCINOMA (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy basal cell carcinoma pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a skin cancer disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes are heavily enriched for components of the Hedgehog signaling axis (PTCH1, SMO, SHH, GLI1, GLI2, SUFU), which is the defining oncogenic driver of basal cell carcinoma. Additional pathway arms include Wnt/beta-catenin signaling components (CTNNB1, WNT family members, FZD receptors), TP53-mediated tumour suppression, CDKN2A/RB1 cell-cycle regulators driving proliferative expansion, and apoptosis modulators (CASP3, BCL2-family), reflecting the major mechanistic arms of the KEGG basal cell carcinoma pathway: Hedgehog-driven initiation and expansion, Wnt co-activation, and TP53 loss enabling tumour progression.
