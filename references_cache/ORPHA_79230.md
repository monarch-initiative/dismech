# ORPHA:79230  HJV or HAMP-related hemochromatosis

**ORPHA:79230** — HJV or HAMP-related hemochromatosis (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Hemochromatosis type 2
- Juvenile hemochromatosis

## Definition

An early-onset and most severe form of rare hemochromatosis characterized by the usual features of hemochromatosis accompanied by cardiomyopathy and hypogonadism.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Adult
- Age of onset: Childhood
