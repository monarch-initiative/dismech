# MYGENESET:KEGG_CHRONIC_MYELOID_LEUKEMIA  KEGG_CHRONIC_MYELOID_LEUKEMIA

**MYGENESET:KEGG_CHRONIC_MYELOID_LEUKEMIA** — KEGG_CHRONIC_MYELOID_LEUKEMIA (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy chronic myeloid leukemia pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a blood cancer disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes encode signaling proteins activated by the BCR-ABL1 fusion tyrosine kinase (the product of the Philadelphia chromosome t(9;22)(q34;q11) translocation), hematopoietic growth-factor receptors, RAS/MAPK proliferative signaling components (RAS, RAF, MAP2K, MAPK), PI3K-AKT survival effectors, JAK-STAT transcriptional activators, and core apoptosis regulators (BCL2-family, CASP3, CYCS), reflecting the major mechanistic arms of the KEGG chronic myeloid leukemia pathway: BCR-ABL1-driven constitutive tyrosine kinase signaling, RAS/MAPK-mediated proliferation, PI3K-AKT survival signaling, JAK-STAT-dependent transcription, and apoptosis evasion.
