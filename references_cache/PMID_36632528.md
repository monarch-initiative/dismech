We report a case of intractable Aspergillus otomycosis with multiple relapses 
despite conventional topical and systemic antifungal treatment, and adjunctive 
usage of hyperbaric oxygen therapy. Of note, otomycosis relapsed even after six 
months of continuous systemic antifungal treatment with therapeutic drug levels 
and without treatment interruption; and only resolved after application of 
topical voriconazole. (max. 75 words).
