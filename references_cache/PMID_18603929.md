**Authors:** Ramachandran U, Malla T, Joshi KS

Meckel-Gruber syndrome.

Ramachandran U(1), Malla T, Joshi KS.

Author information:
(1)Department of Paediatrics, Manipal Teaching Hospital, Pokhara, Nepal.

Meckel- Gruber syndrome is a rare lethal, autosomal disorder. It has been linked 
to chromosome 17. It consists of a triad of occipital meningoencephalocoele, 
large polycystic kidneys and post-axial polydactyly. Death is mainly due to 
pulmonary hypoplasia. We report this rare case which presented with many 
associated defects.
