Inverted Meckel's Diverticulum Causing Adult Intussusception after Blunt Trauma.

Author information:
(1)Department of General Surgery, AdventHealth Orlando, Orlando, FL, USA.

Meckel's diverticulum is an uncommon though well described clinical entity. 
There are few cases of a Meckel's diverticulum having been identified as the 
lead point for adult intussusception. We report the surgical management of a 
45-year-old patient with an inverted Meckel's diverticulum causing distal ileal 
intussusception after blunt abdominal trauma requiring small bowel resection.
