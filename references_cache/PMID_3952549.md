Mesenteric artery aneurysms.

Ferrara BE.

In this case of atherosclerotic aneurysm involving the main superior mesenteric 
artery and an aberrant artery from the aorta, characteristic eggshell 
calcifications were visible on preoperative x-ray films of the abdomen. The 
aneurysm was excised. Flow through the superior mesenteric artery was maintained 
by lateral suture of the artery at the site of excision of the aneurysm.
