## Description

Single-cell marker signature of ventricular cardiomyocytes from human heart (Tucker et al.). Ventricular cardiomyocytes are striated, non-blood muscle cells that drive systolic contraction of the cardiac ventricles via organized sarcomere arrays. They have extremely high mitochondrial content and ATP demand to sustain continuous rhythmic contractile activity throughout life.
