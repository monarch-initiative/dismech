Cogan syndrome is a rare disease characterized by coexisting audiovestibular and 
ocular symptoms. Almost half of patients develop systemic manifestations. We 
report the case of a 38-year-old woman who presented with severe medium and 
large vessel vasculitis as a systemic manifestation of Cogan syndrome.
