# Acquired immunodeficiency syndrome.
**Authors:** Lane HC
**Journal:** Dermatol Clin (1990)

## Content

1. Dermatol Clin. 1990 Oct;8(4):771-86.

Acquired immunodeficiency syndrome.

Lane HC(1).

Author information:
(1)Laboratory of Immunoregulation, National Institute of Allergy and Infectious 
Diseases, National Institutes of Health, Bethesda, Maryland.

The occurrence of unusual infections, in particular Pneumocystis carinii 
pneumonia and the rare neoplasm Kaposi's sarcoma, in previously healthy 
homosexual men in the late 1970s heralded the epidemic of human immunodeficiency 
virus infection, the most severe form of which is the acquired immunodeficiency 
syndrome.
