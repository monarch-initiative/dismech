# Rilonacept.

## Content

1. Rilonacept.

LiverTox®: Clinical and Research Information on Drug-Induced Liver Injury 
[Internet]. Bethesda (MD): National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and 
Kidney Diseases; 2012–.
2020 Apr 20.

Rilonacept is a recombinant interleukin-1 (IL-1) antagonist which is used in the 
therapy of cryopyrin-associated periodic syndromes (CAPS) and other 
autoinflammatory conditions. Rilonacept has had limited clinical use and has yet 
to be linked to cases of clinically apparent, acute liver injury.
