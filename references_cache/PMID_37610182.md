Seroprevalence of Vibrio cholerae in Adults, Haiti, 2017.

Matias WR, Guillaume Y, Augustin GC, Vissieres K, Ternier R, Charles RC, Harris 
JB, Franke MF, Ivers LC.

In Haiti in 2017, the prevalence of serum vibriocidal antibody titers against 
Vibrio cholerae serogroup O1 among adults was 12.4% in Cerca-la-Source and 9.54% 
in Mirebalais, suggesting a high recent prevalence of infection. Improved 
surveillance programs to monitor cholera and guide public health interventions 
in Haiti are necessary.
