# ORPHA:2623  Geleophysic dysplasia

**ORPHA:2623** — Geleophysic dysplasia (Malformation syndrome, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Geleophysic dwarfism

## Definition

A rare skeletal dysplasia characterized by short stature, prominent abnormalities in hands and feet, and a characteristic facial appearance (described as "happy'').

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant
- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Childhood
