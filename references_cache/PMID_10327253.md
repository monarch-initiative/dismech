# Recurrence of fibrochondrogenesis in a consanguineous family.
**Authors:** al-Gazali LI, Bakir M, Dawodu A, Haas D
**Journal:** Clin Dysmorphol (1999)

## Content

1. Clin Dysmorphol. 1999 Jan;8(1):59-61.

Recurrence of fibrochondrogenesis in a consanguineous family.

al-Gazali LI, Bakir M, Dawodu A, Haas D.

A second case of fibrochrondrogenesis in a consanguineous family is described. 
The fact that both sexes are affected in this family supports autosomal 
recessive inheritance.
