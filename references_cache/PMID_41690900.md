Huntington's disease (HD) is usually described as rare, tragic, and intractable. 
Yet, HD offers a strategically unique entry point for neuroscience. With its 
genetic clarity, relatively predictable course, and organized global community, 
HD provides the clearest path to advancing brain repair.
