The manifestations and natural history of spondylo-epi-metaphyseal dysplasia 
with joint laxity.

Beighton P, Gericke G, Kozlowski K, Grobler L.

Spondylo-epi-metaphyseal dysplasia with joint laxity (SEMDJL) is characterized 
by severe dwarfism, articular hypermobility and progressive spinal malalignment. 
The clinical manifestations of 18 affected persons in 13 families of the 
Afrikans-speaking community of South Africa have been analysed and it has become 
evident that survival into adulthood is unusual. SEMDJL is inherited as an 
autosomal recessive trait.
