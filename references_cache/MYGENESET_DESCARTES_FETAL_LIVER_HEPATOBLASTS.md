## Description

Single-cell marker signature of hepatoblasts from human fetal liver (Descartes atlas). Hepatoblasts are bipotential fetal liver progenitors that give rise to hepatocytes and cholangiocytes; the signature is dominated by hepatic metabolic biology reflecting early hepatocyte differentiation.
