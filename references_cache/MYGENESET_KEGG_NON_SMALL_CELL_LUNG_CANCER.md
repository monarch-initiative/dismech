# MYGENESET:KEGG_NON_SMALL_CELL_LUNG_CANCER  KEGG_NON_SMALL_CELL_LUNG_CANCER

**MYGENESET:KEGG_NON_SMALL_CELL_LUNG_CANCER** — KEGG_NON_SMALL_CELL_LUNG_CANCER (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy non-small cell lung cancer pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a cancer disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes represent the major mechanistic arms of NSCLC pathogenesis: EGFR/HER2 activating mutations or amplification driving constitutive receptor tyrosine kinase signalling, KRAS/HRAS/NRAS oncogenic mutation directly activating RAS-RAF-MEK-ERK (MAPK cascade) and PI3K-AKT survival signalling, homozygous deletion of CDKN2A derepressing CDK4/6-CCND1 to override RB1 and drive G1/S transition, and TP53/MDM2 axis abrogating DNA-damage checkpoint responses. NSCLC encompasses adenocarcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and large-cell carcinoma and is distinguished from small-cell lung cancer by its dominant dependence on RTK/RAS oncogene addiction and by the absence of near-universal RB1 biallelic loss.
