Thanatophoric dysplasia type 1 is a lethal congenital anomaly with skeletal 
dysplasia. It is characterized by short limb dwarfism, enlarged head with 
frontal bossing, short neck, protuberant abdomen. It is detected in early 
gestational period by ultrasonography. The other associated anatomical 
abnormalities and characteristic laboratory findings aid in the early diagnosis 
and further work-up.
