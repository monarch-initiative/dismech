# ORPHA:729  Polycythemia vera

**ORPHA:729** — Polycythemia vera (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Acquired primary erythrocytosis
- Osler-Vaquez disease
- PV
- Polycythemia rubra vera
- Vaquez disease

## Definition

A rare acquired myeloproliferative neoplasm characterized by an elevated absolute red blood cell mass (RBCM) caused by uncontrolled red blood cell production, frequently associated with uncontrolled white blood cell and platelet production.

## Inheritance

- Not applicable

## Natural history

- Age of onset: All ages
