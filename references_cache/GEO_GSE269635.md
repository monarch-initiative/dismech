# Dissociation of mitochondrial and ribosomal biogenesis during thallium administration in rat kidney

## Content

Total RNAs from the control and thallium groups were subjected to DNA microarray analysis. Transcriptome analysis showed that many of the genes were upregulated by Tl administration.

We analyzed kidney from rats administered thallium (30 mg/kg, daily for 2 or 5 days) using the ClariomS platform.
