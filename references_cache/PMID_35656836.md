Since May 2022, an outbreak of monkeypox has been ongoing in non-endemic 
countries. We report four cases in Italy in young adult men reporting condomless 
sexual intercourse. The patients are in good clinical condition with no need for 
specific antiviral drugs. Biological samples from seminal fluid were positive 
for monkeypox viral DNA. For many other viruses found in semen there is no 
evidence of sexual transmission. The possibility of sexual transmission of 
monkeypox virus needs to be investigated.
