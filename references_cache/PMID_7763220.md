Discoid lupus erythematosus is a manifestation of chronic cutaneous lupus 
erythematosus with a small risk of systemic involvement. In this review article, 
the role of predisposing factors such as haplotype, hormones, antibodies and 
sunlight are discussed. The clinical features, including variants and 
associations, and management options are presented.
