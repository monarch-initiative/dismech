We present the largest cohort of structured histopathology reports on primary 
ciliary dyskinesia-related chronic rhinosinusitis (PCD-CRS). Despite endoscopic 
differences, PCD-CRS and cystic fibrosis-related chronic rhinosinusitis (CF-CRS) 
had similar structured histopathology reports. Compared to healthy patients and 
those with idiopathic chronic rhinosinusitis without nasal polyps, patients with 
PCD-CRS had an increased neutrophil count.
