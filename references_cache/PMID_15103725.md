# Solitary median maxillary central incisor syndrome: clinical case with a novel mutation of sonic hedgehog.

Solitary median maxillary central incisor (SMMCI) is a rare dental anomaly. It 
is usually considered as a minor manifestation of holoprosencephaly (HPE). Some 
reported families had severe cases of HPE in some members and SMMCI in others. 
Mutations of Sonic Hedgehog (SHH) have been documented in these families. SMMCI 
has also been found as an isolated finding or together with other anomalies such 
as microcephaly, short stature, endocrine pathology, and choanal atresia. We 
describe a patient with SMMCI and a novel SHH mutation: Val332Ala.
