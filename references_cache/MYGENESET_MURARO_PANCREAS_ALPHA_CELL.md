# MYGENESET:MURARO_PANCREAS_ALPHA_CELL  MURARO_PANCREAS_ALPHA_CELL

**MYGENESET:MURARO_PANCREAS_ALPHA_CELL** — MURARO_PANCREAS_ALPHA_CELL (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of pancreatic alpha (A) cells from human pancreas (Muraro et al.). Pancreatic alpha cells are glucagon-secreting endocrine cells located at the periphery of the islets of Langerhans that play an essential role in glucose homeostasis by releasing glucagon in response to hypoglycaemia.
