# MYGENESET:KEGG_AUTOIMMUNE_THYROID_DISEASE  KEGG_AUTOIMMUNE_THYROID_DISEASE

**MYGENESET:KEGG_AUTOIMMUNE_THYROID_DISEASE** — KEGG_AUTOIMMUNE_THYROID_DISEASE (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy autoimmune thyroid disease pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a disease pathway gene set covering both Graves' disease (GD, autoantibody-mediated hyperthyroidism) and Hashimoto's thyroiditis (HT, T-cell-mediated hypothyroidism). Member genes are dominated by MHC class I and II antigen-presentation machinery (HLA-A, HLA-B, HLA-DRA, HLA-DRB1, HLA-DQA1, HLA-DQB1), cytotoxic T-cell effectors (granzymes, perforin), complement components, and immunoglobulin-related genes, reflecting the major mechanistic arms of the KEGG autoimmune thyroid disease pathway: MHC-restricted autoantigen presentation, CD8+ cytotoxic T-cell killing of thyrocytes, B-cell activation and autoantibody production against thyroid autoantigens (thyroglobulin, TPO, TSHR), and complement-mediated cytotoxicity. Shared pathway members with the KEGG type 1 diabetes pathway include much of the MHC and cytotoxic machinery, reflecting the common adaptive-immune core of organ-specific autoimmunity.
