# Achondrogenesis
**Authors:** Amanjot Kaur, M William Keenan
**Journal:** StatPearls (2023)

## Content

Achondrogenesis represents the most severe form of chondrodysplasia and is a lethal skeletal dysplasia. The overall incidence of achondrogenesis is approximately 1 in 40,000 births.
