# Achondrogenesis Type 2 in a Newborn with a Novel Mutation on the COL2A1 Gene.
**Authors:** Ceylan AC, Kocabay G, Karatas Togral A
**Journal:** Balkan J Med Genet (2019)

## Content

Achondrogenesis type 2 is the most severe chondrodysplasia caused by mutations in the COL2A1 gene. It is a rare lethal skeletal dysplasia.
