# MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_CD4_T_CELL  HAY_BONE_MARROW_CD4_T_CELL

**MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_CD4_T_CELL** — HAY_BONE_MARROW_CD4_T_CELL (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of CD4-positive, alpha-beta T (helper) cells from human bone marrow (Hay et al.). CD4+ T helper cells are adaptive immune lymphocytes that recognise peptide-MHC class II complexes on antigen-presenting cells via their T cell receptor (TCR) and coordinate downstream immune responses through cytokine secretion, B cell help, and activation of innate effectors. In bone marrow they exist primarily as resting memory cells.
