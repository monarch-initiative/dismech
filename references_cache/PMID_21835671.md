Until few years ago, Klinefelter syndrome with a homogenous 47.XXY caryotype was 
considered a model of absolute male sterility. In this review, we will discuss 
the fertility issue following TEsticular Sperm Extraction-IntraCytoplasmic Sperm 
Injection (TESE-ICSI) and the potential advantage of searching for and 
cryopreserving spermatozoa in adolescent instead of adult patients.
