Author information:
(1)Crippled Children's Division, Oregon Health Sciences University, Portland

An international survey of the long term results of treating galactosaemia has 
shown poor results. These do not seem to be related to any of the relevant 
variables studied, for example delayed diagnosis or poor dietary compliance.
