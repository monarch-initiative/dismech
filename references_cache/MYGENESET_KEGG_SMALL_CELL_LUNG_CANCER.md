# MYGENESET:KEGG_SMALL_CELL_LUNG_CANCER  KEGG_SMALL_CELL_LUNG_CANCER

**MYGENESET:KEGG_SMALL_CELL_LUNG_CANCER** — KEGG_SMALL_CELL_LUNG_CANCER (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy small cell lung cancer pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a cancer disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes represent the major mechanistic arms of SCLC pathogenesis: universal biallelic loss of RB1 and TP53 driving cell-cycle deregulation and checkpoint abrogation, BCL2-family overexpression conferring anti-apoptotic survival, integrin and ECM components (ITGA/ITGB subunits, collagen IV chains, laminin subunits, fibronectin) mediating cell-adhesion-based drug resistance (CAM-DR), and MYC amplification with PI3K/AKT signaling driving proliferative expansion. SCLC is a high-grade neuroendocrine carcinoma with near-universal initial chemosensitivity but rapid acquired resistance, reflected in the pathway's emphasis on ECM-mediated survival signaling alongside the canonical cell-cycle and apoptosis machinery.
