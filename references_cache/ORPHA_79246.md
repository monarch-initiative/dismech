# ORPHA:79246  Pyruvate dehydrogenase phosphatase deficiency

**ORPHA:79246** — Pyruvate dehydrogenase phosphatase deficiency (Clinical subtype, Subtype of disorder)

## Synonyms

- PDH phosphatase deficiency

## Definition

Pyruvate dehydrogenase phosphatase deficiency is a very rare subtype of pyruvate dehydrogenase deficiency (PDHD) characterized by lactic acidemia in the neonatal period.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Infancy
- Age of onset: Neonatal
