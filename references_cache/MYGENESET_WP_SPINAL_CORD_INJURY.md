**MYGENESET:WP_SPINAL_CORD_INJURY** — WP_SPINAL_CORD_INJURY (msigdb, C2:CP:WIKIPATHWAYS)

## Description

WikiPathways community-curated spinal cord injury pathway gene set (MSigDB C2:CP:WIKIPATHWAYS). The 117-member set captures the major molecular arms of traumatic SCI pathophysiology: acute neuroinflammation mediated by innate immune cells and inflammatory cytokines; secondary neuronal apoptosis driven by caspase activation and Bcl-2 family signalling; reactive astrogliosis and glial scar formation; deposition of inhibitory chondroitin sulphate proteoglycans (CSPGs) in the extracellular matrix that impede axon regeneration; and the attempted but largely abortive axon regeneration response including growth-cone dynamics and axon-guidance signalling.
