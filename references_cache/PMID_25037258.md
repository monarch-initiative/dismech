Severe drug-induced dermatoses.

Ahronowitz I, Fox L(1).

Author information:
(1)Department of Dermatology, University of California San Francisco, CA USA. 
Email: foxli@derm.ucsf.edu.

A variety of common dermatoses are known to have drug-induced variants. This 
article discusses the clinical presentation, time frames, reported culprit 
medications, pathophysiology and management of drug-induced lupus, cutaneous 
vasculitis, pemphigus, pemphigoid, linear IgA bullous dermatosis, Sweet's 
syndrome, erythema nodosum, pyoderma gangrenosum, pseudolymphoma, lichen planus, 
and psoriasis.
