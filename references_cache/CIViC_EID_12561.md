## Evidence statement

In the phase III randomized open-label CheckMate 8HW trial (NCT04008030; n = 303, 255 centrally confirmed MSI-H/dMMR metastatic colorectal cancer), nivolumab plus ipilimumab significantly improved progression-free survival versus investigator’s choice of chemotherapy (mFOLFOX6 or FOLFIRI with or without bevacizumab or cetuximab) as first-line treatment (24-month PFS 72%, 95% CI 64–79 vs 14%, 95% CI 6–25; p < 0.001). The restricted mean survival time at 24 months was 10.6 months longer (95% CI 8.4–12.9) with nivolumab plus ipilimumab, and grade 3–4 treatment-related adverse events occurred in 23% vs 48% of patients, respectively.
