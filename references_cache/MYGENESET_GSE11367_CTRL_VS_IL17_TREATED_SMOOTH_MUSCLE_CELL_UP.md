## Description

Genes upregulated in human smooth muscle cells treated with interleukin-17 (IL-17) versus untreated control (GSE11367, MSigDB C7 immunologic-signature contrast). Reflects the transcriptional response of smooth muscle cells to IL-17-driven inflammatory signaling, including chemokine production, NF-kappaB activation, and neutrophil-recruiting responses.
