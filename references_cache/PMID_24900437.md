Structure-activity relationship analysis in a series of
3-(5-((2-oxoindolin-3-ylidene)methyl)furan-2-yl)amides identified compound 13, a
pan-Pim kinases inhibitor with excellent biochemical potency and kinase
selectivity. Compound 13 exhibited in vitro synergy with chemotherapeutics and
robust in vivo efficacy in two Pim kinases driven tumor models.
