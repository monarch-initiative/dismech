Author information:
(1)From the Department of Health Sciences Research, Mayo Clinic, Scottsdale, AZ.

Lynch syndrome was described over a century ago but information on the medical 
consequences and optimal management of this disorder continue to amass and 
evolve. This brief overview highlights the gene-specific and site-specific 
cancer penetrance and management options for those with Lynch syndrome.
