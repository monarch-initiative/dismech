# ORPHA:803  Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis

**ORPHA:803** — Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- ALS
- Charcot disease
- Lou Gehrig disease

## Definition

A neurodegenerative disease characterized by progressive muscular paralysis reflecting degeneration of motor neurons in the primary motor cortex, corticospinal tracts, brainstem and spinal cord.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant
- Autosomal recessive
- Not applicable

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adult
