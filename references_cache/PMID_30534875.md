Ehlers Danlos Syndrome comprises a heterogeneous group of genetic disorders of 
the connective tissue, due to defects in collagen or its modifying enzymes. We 
report a 21 years old male presenting with translucent skin revealing the 
subcutaneous venous pattern. He had a thin face, large-appearing eyes, thin 
lips, thin nose, joint hypermotility and history of hip dysplasia. A vascular 
Ehlers Danlos Syndrome was suspected. However, the genetic study to confirm the 
diagnosis was not done.
