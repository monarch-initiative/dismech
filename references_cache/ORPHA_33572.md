# ORPHA:33572  5-oxoprolinase deficiency

**ORPHA:33572** — 5-oxoprolinase deficiency (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Oxoprolinuria due to oxoprolinase deficiency

## Definition

A very heterogeneous condition characterized by 5-oxoprolinuria.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Infancy
