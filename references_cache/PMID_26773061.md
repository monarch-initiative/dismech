# New players in heterochromatin silencing: histone variant H3.3 and the ATRX/DAXX chaperone.

A number of studies have demonstrated that various components of the 
ATRX/DAXX/Histone H3.3 complex are important for heterochromatin silencing at 
multiple genomic regions. We provide an overview of the individual components 
(ATRX, DAXX and/or H3.3) tested in each study and propose a model where the 
ATRX/DAXX chaperone complex deposits H3.3 to maintain the H3K9me3 modification 
at heterochromatin throughout the genome.
