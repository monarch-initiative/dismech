Plain Language Summary: A 25-year-old male presented with chronic watery 
diarrhea and severe intestinal failure due to collagenous sprue. Treatments, 
including immunosuppressants and a gluten-free diet, were ineffective. 
Tacrolimus shows promise in treating refractory cases.
