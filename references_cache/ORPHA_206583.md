# ORPHA:206583  Adult polyglucosan body disease

**ORPHA:206583** — Adult polyglucosan body disease (Clinical subtype, Subtype of disorder)

## Synonyms

- APBD

## Definition

A glycogen storage disease of adults characterized by progressive upper and lower motor neuron dysfunction, progressive neurogenic bladder and cognitive difficulties that can lead to dementia.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adult
