# Antenatal Bartter syndrome.
**Authors:** Afzal M, Khan EA, Khan WA, Anwar V, Yaqoob A, Rafiq S, Azam S
**Journal:** J Coll Physicians Surg Pak (2014)

## Content

1. J Coll Physicians Surg Pak. 2014 May;24 Suppl 2:S121-3.

Antenatal Bartter syndrome.

Afzal M(1), Khan EA(1), Khan WA(1), Anwar V(1), Yaqoob A(1), Rafiq S(1), Azam 
S(2).

Author information:
(1)Department of Paediatrics, Quaid-e-Azam International Hospital, Islamabad.
(2)Department of Obstetrician, Quaid-e-Azam International Hospital, Islamabad.

Antenatal Bartter syndrome is characterized by severe polyhydramnios in mother 
leading to premature delivery. Antenatal treatment has proven effective to 
prevent these problems. Postnatally newborns suffer from recurrent episodes of 
severe dehydration and electrolyte imbalance which can lead to fatal outcome. 
These manifestations are likely to be overlooked and missed under the umbrella 
of diagnosis of prematurity. This premature newborn with antenatal 
polyhydramnios had severe manifestations of polyuria, recurrent dehydration, 
electrolyte derangements and metabolic alkalosis. She was managed accordingly 
but unfortunately could not survive beyond 4 weeks.
