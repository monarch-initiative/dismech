# Variable phenotypic expression of Apert syndrome in monozygotic twins
**Authors:** Matthieu Dap, Pascale Bach‐Segura, Charline Bertholdt, Didier Menzies, Jean‐Pierre Masutti, Olivier Klein, Estelle Perdriolle‐Galet, Laetitia Lambert, Olivier Morel
**Journal:** Clinical Case Reports (2019)
**DOI:** [10.1002/ccr3.1915](https://doi.org/10.1002/ccr3.1915)

## Content

Key Clinical MessageApert syndrome in monozygotic twins can lead to different phenotypic expression of the disease in the two fetuses. Apert syndrome can be associated with congenital left diaphragmatic hernia and cleft palate.
