**MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_NK_CELLS** — HAY_BONE_MARROW_NK_CELLS (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of natural killer (NK) cells from human bone marrow (Hay et al.). NK cells are innate lymphocytes that kill virus-infected and transformed target cells via granule exocytosis (perforin/granzyme) and death receptor pathways without prior sensitization.
