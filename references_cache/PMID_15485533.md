Author information:
(1)Dermatology, New Jersey Medical School, Newark, New Jersey, USA.

Abstract Parvovirus B19 infections cause a wide range of clinical findings, 
including erythema infectiosum, "glove-and-socks" syndrome, arthropathies, red 
cell aplasia, and intrauterine infections. The condition can be diagnosed 
serologically. The prognosis is excellent in healthy, immunocompetent 
individuals.
