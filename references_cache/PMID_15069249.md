Comment in
    Neurol India. 2004 Jun;52(2):271; author reply 271.

Satoyoshi syndrome (Komuragaeri disease) is a rare disorder of presumed 
autoimmune etiology, characterized by painful muscle spasms, alopecia, diarrhea, 
endocrinopathy with amenorrhoea and secondary skeletal abnormalities. Most of 
the previous reports are of the Japanese people. We report the first case from 
India.
