# Heme-induced neutrophil extracellular traps contribute to the pathogenesis of sickle cell disease
**Authors:** Grace Chen, Dachuan Zhang, Tobias A. Fuchs, Deepa Manwani, Denisa D. Wagner, Paul S. Frenette
**Journal:** Blood (2014)
**DOI:** [10.1182/blood-2013-10-529982](https://doi.org/10.1182/blood-2013-10-529982)

## Content

Key Points
NETs are present and pathogenic in sickle cell disease. Plasma heme and proinflammatory cytokines collaborate to activate release of NETs.
