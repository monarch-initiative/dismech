# Single-nuclei RNA-seq of meningioma

## Content

We applied single-nuclei RNA-seq to meningioma samples across grades and methylation classes, in order to characterize features of meningioma heterogeneity across subgroups. We identified an overall decrease in myeloid cell proportions from grade 1 to grade 3 meningiomas, as well as a phenotypic shift from microglia-like cells towards a myeloid-derived proliferative macrophage phenotype.
