# ORPHA:640  Hereditary neuropathy with liability to pressure palsies

**ORPHA:640** — Hereditary neuropathy with liability to pressure palsies (Malformation syndrome, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Current pressure-sensitive neuropathy
- HNPP
- Heterozygous microdeletion 17p11.2p12
- Potato-grubbing palsy
- Tomaculous neuropathy
- Tulip-bulb digger's palsy

## Definition

A rare neurologic disease characterized by recurrent mononeuropathies usually triggered by minor physical activities innocuous to healthy people.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Adult
- Age of onset: Childhood
- Age of onset: Elderly
- Age of onset: Infancy
