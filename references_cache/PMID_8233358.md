Cataracts in Morquio syndrome (mucopolysaccharidosis IV A).

Olsen H(1), Baggesen K, Sjolie AK.

Author information:
(1)Department of Ophthalmology, Aarhus University Hospital, Denmark.

Three siblings with Morquio syndrome (mucopolysaccharidosis IV A) are described. 
In addition to the characteristic dwarfism with skeletal deformities, odontoid 
anomalies, hearing loss and corneal clouding, the authors found almost identical 
lens opacities in all three patients. Lenticular opacities have not previously 
been described in patients with Morquio's syndrome IV A.
