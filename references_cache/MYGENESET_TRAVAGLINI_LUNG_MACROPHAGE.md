## Description

Single-cell marker signature of alveolar macrophages from human lung (Travaglini et al.). Alveolar macrophages are tissue-resident phagocytes that patrol the alveolar space, phagocytose inhaled particulate matter, apoptotic cells, and spent surfactant aggregates. They serve as frontline innate immune sentinels, regulate local inflammation, and maintain alveolar lipid and surfactant homeostasis.
