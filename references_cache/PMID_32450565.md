Various cardiovascular complications have been reported in patients with 
coronavirus disease 2019. Common complications include acute myocardial injury, 
myocarditis, arrhythmia, pericarditis, heart failure, and shock. We present a 
case of cor pulmonale diagnosed with serial point of care ultrasound. Given the 
current shortage of personal protective equipment (PPE) and high infectivity of 
this virus, we acknowledge the utility of this tool in obtaining important 
clinical information while minimizing exposure and PPE consumption.
