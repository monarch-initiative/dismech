## Evidence statement

In the phase III randomized open-label CheckMate 8HW trial (NCT04008030; n = 707 randomized; centrally confirmed MSI-H/dMMR: n = 296 nivolumab+ipilimumab vs 286 nivolumab) showed nivolumab plus ipilimumab significantly improved progression-free survival versus nivolumab monotherapy across all treatment lines (HR 0.62, 95% CI 0.48–0.81; p = 0.0003). Median PFS was not reached (95% CI 53.8-NE) with nivolumab plus ipilimumab and 39.3 months (22.1-NE) with nivolumab. Objective response rate was 71% (95% CI 65–76) vs 58% (95% CI 52–64; p = 0.0011), with complete responses in 30% and 28%, respectively. Grade ≥3 AEs occurred in 22% vs 14%, including diarrhea/colitis (3%), hypophysitis (3%), and adrenal insufficiency (3%), with three treatment-related deaths reported (2 myocarditis/pneumonitis in combination arm, 1 pneumonitis in monotherapy arm).
