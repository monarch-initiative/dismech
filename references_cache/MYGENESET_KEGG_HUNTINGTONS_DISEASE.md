# MYGENESET:KEGG_HUNTINGTONS_DISEASE  KEGG_HUNTINGTONS_DISEASE

**MYGENESET:KEGG_HUNTINGTONS_DISEASE** — KEGG_HUNTINGTONS_DISEASE (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy Huntington's disease pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes are heavily enriched for mitochondrial electron-transport-chain subunits (oxidative phosphorylation), transcriptional coactivators and repressors (CBP/CREBBP, SP1, NCOR, HDAC families reflecting transcriptional dysregulation), and apoptosis regulators, reflecting the major mechanistic arms of the KEGG Huntington's disease pathway representation. Polyglutamine-expanded mutant huntingtin (HTT) drives intranuclear inclusion body formation, and the resulting transcriptional dysregulation and mitochondrial impairment converge on striatal neuron death. As a legacy/core pathway it captures established mechanism and omits more recently characterised arms such as selective autophagy/mitophagy.
