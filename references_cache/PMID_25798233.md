Human chondrodysplasias are a group of conditions that affect the cartilage. 
This review is focused on the involvement of transforming growth factor-β 
signaling in a group of chondrodysplasias, entitled acromelic dysplasia, 
characterized by short stature, short hands and restricted joint mobility.
