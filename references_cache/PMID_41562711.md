In this study we evaluated the expression of Angiopoietin 1, Angiopoietin 2, and 
Tie2 by immunohistochemistry in the skin of 10 patients with erythemato 
telangiectatic and papulopustular rosacea. Significantly increased expression of 
Tie2 and Angiopoietin 2 in the endothelial cells of the dermal vessels in 
rosacea skin vs. non-lesional skin (100% and 33.3% for Tie2, and 100% and 50% 
for Angiopoietin 2) was observed. There was no difference in the expression of 
Angiopoietin 1 and phosphorylated Tie2 (pTie2) between the lesional skin of 
rosacea and non-lesional skin.
