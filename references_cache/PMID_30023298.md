# Incidence of maple syrup urine disease, propionic acidemia, and methylmalonic aciduria from newborn screening data
**Authors:** Kimberly A Chapman et al.
**Journal:** Mol Genet Metab Rep (2018)
**DOI:** [10.1016/j.ymgmr.2018.03.011](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ymgmr.2018.03.011)

## Content

Incidence for the branched-chain intoxication-type disorders, maple syrup urine disease, propionic acidemia and methlymalonic aciduria is dependent on the population screened.

Maple syrup urine disease incidence in the United States was calculated to be 1: 220219, in South-West Germany 1: 119573 (Germany nationwide 1:177978), and in Kuwait 1: 59426.
