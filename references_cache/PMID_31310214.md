1. Emerg Infect Dis. 2019 Aug;25(8):1581-1583. doi: 10.3201/eid2508.181690.

Bejel, a Nonvenereal Treponematosis, among Men Who Have Sex with Men, Japan.

Kawahata T, Kojima Y, Furubayashi K, Shinohara K, Shimizu T, Komano J, Mori H,
Motomura K.

Bejel, an endemic treponematosis caused by infection with Treponema pallidum
subspecies endemicum, has not been reported in eastern Asia and the Pacific
region. We report local spread of bejel among men who have sex with men in
Japan. Spread was complicated by venereal syphilis.
