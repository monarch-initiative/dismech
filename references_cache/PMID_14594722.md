# LDA--a java-based linkage disequilibrium analyzer.
**Authors:** Ding K, Zhou K, He F, Shen Y
**Journal:** Bioinformatics (2003)
**DOI:** [10.1093/bioinformatics/btg276](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btg276)

## Content

1. Bioinformatics. 2003 Nov 1;19(16):2147-8. doi: 10.1093/bioinformatics/btg276.

LDA--a java-based linkage disequilibrium analyzer.

Ding K(1), Zhou K, He F, Shen Y.

Author information:
(1)Institute of Basic Medical Sciences, Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences (and 
Peking Union Medical College, National Laboratory of Medical Molecular Biology, 
Beijing, 100005, People's Republic of China.

We describe an integrated java-based program that provides elaborate graphic and 
plain-text output of pairwise linkage disequilibrium (LD) analysis of single 
nucleotide polymorphisms genotypic data. It is most suitable for molecular 
geneticists, who are focusing on LD measures estimation, statistical 
significance test and extent prediction.
AVAILABILITY: The software is available at: http://www.chgb.org.cn/lda/lda.htm.
SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Detailed tutorials, LDA help system and examples are 
distributed within LDA software. For Macintosh OS X user, the Jre version 1.4 
can be downloaded from http://connect.apple.com.
