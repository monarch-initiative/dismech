# Neutrophil extracellular traps enhance macrophage killing of bacterial pathogens
**Authors:** Andrew J. Monteith, Jeanette M. Miller, C. Noel Maxwell, Walter J. Chazin, Eric P. Skaar
**Journal:** Science Advances (2021)
**DOI:** [10.1126/sciadv.abj2101](https://doi.org/10.1126/sciadv.abj2101)

## Content

S100A9 regulates mitochondrial-mediated neutrophil extracellular trap formation, which augments macrophage bactericidal activity.
