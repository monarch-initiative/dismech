    Nat Cell Biol. 2012 Dec;14(12):1282-94. doi: 10.1038/ncb2628.

Basal cell carcinoma has been shown to originate from activation of hedgehog
signalling in interfollicular epidermal progenitor cells. Analyses of the early
steps of basal cell carcinoma formation show that this process requires
reprogramming of interfolliclular epidermal cells to an embryonic hair follicle
progenitor-like fate, with concomitant Wnt pathway activation.
