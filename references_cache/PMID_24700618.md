# Epidemiology of fragile X syndrome: a systematic review and meta-analysis
**Authors:** Jessica Hunter et al.
**Journal:** Am J Med Genet A (2014)
**DOI:** [10.1002/ajmg.a.36511](https://doi.org/10.1002/ajmg.a.36511)

## Content

Prevalence estimates for fragile X syndrome vary considerably. This systematic review and meta-analysis was conducted to provide an accurate prevalence estimate for this disorder using primary publications in PubMed, Embase, and the Cochrane library.

Using the random-effects model, frequency of the full mutation was 1.4 (95% CI: 0.1-3.1) per 10,000 males and 0.9 (95% CI: 0.0-2.9) per 10,000 females (1:7,143 and 1:11,111, respectively) in the total population.
