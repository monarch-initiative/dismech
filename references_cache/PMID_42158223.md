Myalgic encephalomyelitis/chronic fatigue syndrome (ME/CFS) is a complex 
neuroimmunological disorder characterized by disabling symptoms that are often 
difficult to manage. More recently, in the context of SARS-CoV-2 infection, 
potential pathophysiological overlaps and disease modulation have been 
hypothesized. Our successful case highlights the need to investigate novel 
therapeutic approaches, including plasma exchange.
