Hirschsprung disease (HD) can be associated with congenital central 
hypoventilation syndrome (CCHS). CCHS is mostly due to PHOX2B pathogenic 
variants. First report of HD on fetal autopsy leading to CCHS diagnosis. 
Highlights the role of fetal autopsy in stillborn babies, to guide genetic 
investigation and refine genetic counseling.
