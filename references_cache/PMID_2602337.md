Weakness, numbness, tingling and multiple sclerosis.

Heron J.

Multiple sclerosis is the most common serious neurological disease in young 
patients but it is not the only cause of paraesthesiae. Such sensory symptoms 
occur frequently and reflect a variety of underlying conditions.
