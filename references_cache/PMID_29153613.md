Neuroinflammation may be central in epileptogenesis. In this study we analysed
inflammatory reaction markers in brain tissue of Mesial Temporal Lobe Epilepsy
with Hippocampal Sclerosis (MTLE-HS) patients. TLR4, IL-1β and IL-10 gene
expression as well as the presence of activated HLA-DR+ microglia was evaluated
in 23 patients and 10 cadaveric controls. Inflammation characterized by the
presence of HLA-DR+ microglia and TLR4, IL-1β overexpression was evident in
hippocampus and anterior temporal cortex of MTLE-HS patients. Anti-inflammatory
IL-10 was also overexpressed in MTLE-HS patients. Our results show that
hippocampal neuroinflammation extends beyond lesional limits, as far as the
anterior temporal cortex.
