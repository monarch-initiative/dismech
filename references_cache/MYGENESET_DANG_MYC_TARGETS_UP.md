# MYGENESET:DANG_MYC_TARGETS_UP  DANG_MYC_TARGETS_UP

**MYGENESET:DANG_MYC_TARGETS_UP** — DANG_MYC_TARGETS_UP (msigdb, C2:CGP)

## Description

MSigDB C2:CGP gene set of genes up-regulated by the MYC oncogenic transcription factor and whose promoters are directly bound by MYC, as defined by Zeller and Dang et al. (2003, PMID:14519204). This is a genetic-perturbation response signature (MYC oncogene activation, UP component), not a cell-type signature: member genes are induced across diverse cellular contexts by enforced MYC expression and reflect the canonical MYC transcriptional programme, which centres on ribosome biogenesis, rRNA processing and modification, translation, nucleotide biosynthesis, and cell cycle entry. The 130 member genes were prioritised by transcriptomic evidence of MYC responsiveness and validated by chromatin immunoprecipitation of MYC promoter binding.
