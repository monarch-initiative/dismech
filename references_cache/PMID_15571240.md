Author information:
(1)Department of Gynecology, Perinatology and Child Health, University of Roma
La Sapienza, Roma, Italy.

An homology model of human adenylosuccinate lyase structure shows that P100A
substitution distorts the amino acid chain of domain I in the proximity of
His-86, which behaves as general acid in the catalysis, and may expose Cys-98
and Cys-99 to oxidising agents. This model is in line with the observation that
the defective protein is strongly inhibited by 4-hydroxy-2-nonenal, an
hydroxyalkenal that is known to form thio-ether linkage with proteins.
