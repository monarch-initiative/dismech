# Disorders associated with osteopoikilosis: 5 different lesions in a family.

Although osteopoikilosis is generally considered an accidental finding, several 
developmental dysplasias coexisting with this disorder have been reported. 
However, all authors have mentioned only one coexisting finding, and most of 
them are case reports. We report a family in whom various members had 
osteopoikilosis with 5 different associated lesions. We suggest that 
osteopoikilosis is a bone manifestation of a generalized fibroproliferative or 
stenosing disease.
