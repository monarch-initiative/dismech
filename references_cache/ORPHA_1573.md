# ORPHA:1573  Hypotrichosis with juvenile macular degeneration

**ORPHA:1573** — Hypotrichosis with juvenile macular degeneration (Malformation syndrome, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- HJMD
- Hypotrichosis with juvenile macular dystrophy

## Definition

Hypotrichosis with juvenile macular degeneration (HJMD) is a very rare syndrome characterized by sparse and short hair from birth followed by progressive macular degeneration leading to blindness.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Infancy
- Age of onset: Neonatal
