# Male siblings with tibia-metacarpal type of chondrodysplasia punctata without maternal factors.
**Authors:** Honda R, Ichiyama T, Maeba S, Sunagawa S, Furukawa S
**Journal:** Brain Dev (2008)
**DOI:** [10.1016/j.braindev.2007.09.003](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.braindev.2007.09.003)

Male siblings with tibia-metacarpal type of chondrodysplasia punctata without

Honda R(1), Ichiyama T, Maeba S, Sunagawa S, Furukawa S.

Author information:
(1)Department of Pediatrics, Yamaguchi University Graduate School of Medicine, 
Ube, Yamaguchi, Japan. honda_accordjp@yahoo.co.jp

We report male siblings with tibia-metacarpal type of chondrodysplasia punctata, 
which was diagnosed by radiographic findings and clinical appearances. This is 
the first report of siblings without maternal systemic lupus erythematosus or 
medications during both pregnancies.
