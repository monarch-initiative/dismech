**MYGENESET:JISON_SICKLE_CELL_DISEASE_UP** — JISON_SICKLE_CELL_DISEASE_UP (msigdb, C2:CGP)

## Description

Genes up-regulated in peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMC) from sickle cell disease patients compared to healthy subjects (Jison et al. 2004, PMID:15031206). The 183-gene upregulated signature captures the chronic inflammatory, oxidant, and hemolytic stress state of steady-state sickle cell disease, with enrichment for transcripts involved in heme metabolism, antioxidant and stress responses, inflammation, leukocyte activation, and innate immune signalling.
