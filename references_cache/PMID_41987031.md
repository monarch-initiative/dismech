# Whole-Genome Analysis of Treponema pallidum Subspecies endemicum among Men Who Have Sex with Men, Japan, 2020-2023.
**Authors:** Ohama Y, Imai K, Kotaka Y, Lee K, Itoda I, Nakayama SI, Ohnishi M, Akeda Y
**Journal:** Emerg Infect Dis (2026)
**DOI:** [10.3201/eid3204.251045](https://doi.org/10.3201/eid3204.251045)

## Content

1. Emerg Infect Dis. 2026 Apr;32(4):636-639. doi: 10.3201/eid3204.251045.

Whole-Genome Analysis of Treponema pallidum Subspecies endemicum among Men Who
Have Sex with Men, Japan, 2020-2023.

Ohama Y, Imai K, Kotaka Y, Lee K, Itoda I, Nakayama SI, Ohnishi M, Akeda Y.

Whole-genome sequencing of Treponema pallidum subsp. endemicum strains from men
who have sex with men in Japan revealed a genetically distinct lineage from
other geographic regions circulating via sexual transmission. Strengthening
global molecular epidemiologic surveillance is essential for clarifying
epidemiologic trends, clinical characteristics, and transmission pathways of
this subspecies.
