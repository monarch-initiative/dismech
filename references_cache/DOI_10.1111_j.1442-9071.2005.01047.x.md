# Acute annular outer retinopathy
**Authors:** Yoshinori Mitamura, Hiroki Ito, Yasushi Nakamura, Osamutaro Fujiwara, Kenji Ohtsuka
**Journal:** Clinical &amp; Experimental Ophthalmology (2005)
**DOI:** [10.1111/j.1442-9071.2005.01047.x](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1442-9071.2005.01047.x)

## Content

AbstractAcute annular outer retinopathy is an uncommon disorder of uncertain aetiology characterized by a unilateral visual field defect with an irregular annular band of grey‐white, deep retinal opacification in a peripapillary location. Acute annular outer retinopathy is considered to be a variant of acute zonal occult outer retinopathy. The case is reported of acute annular outer retinopathy with spontaneous good visual recovery and no ophthalmoscopic sequelae.
