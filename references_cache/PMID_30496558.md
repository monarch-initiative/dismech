Olarte L(1).

Author information:
(1)Division of Infectious Diseases, Children's Mercy Hospital, Kansas City,

The use of empiric vancomycin plus a third-generation cephalosporin for 
suspected bacterial meningitis has been recommended since 1997. Although the 
prevalence of ceftriaxone-nonsusceptible pneumococcal meningitis has decreased, 
vancomycin should still be included as empiric therapy for bacterial meningitis.
