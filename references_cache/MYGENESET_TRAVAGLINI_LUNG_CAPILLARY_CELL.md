**MYGENESET:TRAVAGLINI_LUNG_CAPILLARY_CELL** — TRAVAGLINI_LUNG_CAPILLARY_CELL (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of capillary endothelial cells from human lung (Travaglini et al., 2020). Capillary endothelial cells are thin, flattened cells lining the smallest blood vessels of the lung, forming the inner surface of the pulmonary capillary bed. They are central to pulmonary angiogenesis, vascular barrier integrity, and the regulation of fluid and solute exchange across the blood-air interface.
