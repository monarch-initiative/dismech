# Acute lung injury due to cadmium inhalation--a case report.
**Authors:** Panchal L, Vaideeswar P
**Journal:** Indian J Pathol Microbiol (2006)

## Content

1. Indian J Pathol Microbiol. 2006 Apr;49(2):265-6.

Acute lung injury due to cadmium inhalation--a case report.

Panchal L(1), Vaideeswar P.

Author information:
(1)Department of Pathology, Cardiovascular & Thoracic Division, Seth G. S. 
Medical College, Parel, Mumbai.

Heavy metal inhalation is a rare cause of acute lung injury. Among the various 
heavy metals, cadmium is more commonly known to cause acute lung injury. A case 
of accidental inhalation of cadmium fumes in a young male is presented. The 
incident occurred in local silver jewellery manufacturing unit.
