# ORPHA:733  Familial adenomatous polyposis

**ORPHA:733** — Familial adenomatous polyposis (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Colorectal adenomatous polyposis
- FAP
- Familial polyposis coli

## Definition

A rare intestinal polyposis syndrome characterized by the development of up to hundreds to thousands of adenomas in the large bowel. It is a multi-system disorder with important extracolonic manifestations.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant
- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adult
