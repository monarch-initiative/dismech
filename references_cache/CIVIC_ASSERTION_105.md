## Summary

Non-small cell lung cancer with EGFR L858R mutation is sensitive to gefitinib.

## Description

L858R is among the most common sensitizing EGFR mutations in NSCLC, and is assessed via DNA mutational analysis including Sanger sequencing and next generation sequencing methods. Tyrosine kinase inhibitor gefitinib is associated with improved progression free survival over chemotherapy in EGFR L858R patients.
