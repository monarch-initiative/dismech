Ehlers-Danlos-Tschernogobow : Histoire contrarlée de la maladie.

[Article in French]

Hamonet C, Ducret L, Baeza-Velasco CL, Layadi K.

Ehlers-Danlos disease had a paradoxical fate. Clinically identified 123 years 
ago, it is unknown to nearly all present doctors. Often described for its 
dramatic aspects, diagnostic confusion with fibromyalgia in particular, 
ignorance of effective treatments such as oxygen therapy and orthotics are new 
concepts that should shake the prejudices derived from the history of this 
disease.
