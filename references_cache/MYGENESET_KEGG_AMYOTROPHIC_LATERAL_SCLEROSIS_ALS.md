## Description

KEGG legacy amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. ALS is a fatal motor neuron disease characterised by progressive degeneration of upper and lower motor neurons. The member genes reflect the major mechanistic arms represented in the KEGG ALS pathway: SOD1-mediated superoxide detoxification and oxidative stress, glutamate excitotoxicity through ionotropic (AMPA/NMDA) receptors, neurofilament accumulation and cytoskeletal disruption, mitochondrial dysfunction, and the downstream apoptotic execution of motor neurons. As a legacy/core pathway it captures the dominant established mechanisms as of the time of KEGG pathway assembly.
