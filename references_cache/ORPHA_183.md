# ORPHA:183  Eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangiitis

**ORPHA:183** — Eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangiitis (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Churg-Strauss syndrome
- EGPA
- Granulomatous allergic angiitis

## Definition

A rare systemic vasculitis of small vessels characterized by asthma, blood and tissue eosinophilia and vasculitis manifestations.

## Inheritance

- Not applicable

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Adult
- Age of onset: Elderly
