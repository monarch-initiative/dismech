Molecular Characterization of Echinococcus vogeli from Human Case, Colombia, 
2024.

Morcillo Muñoz JA, Rodríguez-Lugo DA, Moreno JH, Astorquiza MH, Patiño LH, 
Cáceres TM, Ramírez AL, Ramírez JD, Faccini-Martínez ÁA.

In Colombia, 35 confirmed cases of neotropical polycystic echinococcosis were 
reported during 1978-2018. In most cases, Echinococcus vogeli was identified by 
means of morphologic identification. We describe a case of E. vogeli 
echinococcosis in a woman, diagnosed through PCR, mitochondrial DNA sequencing, 
and molecular characterization.
