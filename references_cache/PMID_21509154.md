# Alternative pop-up for surfers with low back pain.
**Authors:** Hammer RL, Loubert PV
**Journal:** N Am J Sports Phys Ther (2010)

## Content

1. N Am J Sports Phys Ther. 2010 Feb;5(1):15-8.

Alternative pop-up for surfers with low back pain.

Hammer RL(1), Loubert PV.

Author information:
(1)College of Health Professions, Central Michigan University, Mount Pleasant, 
MI.

Surfing is enjoyed by many people around the world. A common problem in surfers 
is back pain during the "take-off," specifically the "pop-up." This article 
describes each part of the "take-off, and introduces an alternative to the 
"prone pop up" - called the "knee pop-up." This alternative is a suggested 
technique to alleviate the stress in the lumbar spine during surfing.
