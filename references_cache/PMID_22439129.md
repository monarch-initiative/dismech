Author information:
(1)Department of Radiodiagnosis, Government Medical College, Nagpur, India.

Fibrochondrogenesis is a rare, neonatally lethal osteochondrodysplasia, with 
autosomal recessive inheritance. It differs from other lethal dwarfisms in that 
it leads to broad, long-bone metaphyses (dumb-bell shaped) and pear-shaped 
vertebral bodies. We report a case of fibrochondrogenesis with severe 
pear-shaped platyspondyly, suspected antenatally, and give a comprehensive 
pictorial review of the antenatal ultrasound and postnatal radiographic 
findings. Only few cases of fibrochondrogenesis are diagnosed before the 
termination of pregnancy.
