Multisystem Inflammatory Syndrome in Children after SARS-CoV-2 Vaccination.

Jain E, Donowitz JR, Aarons E, Marshall BC, Miller MP.

Multisystem inflammatory syndrome in children (MIS-C) is a hyperinflammatory 
state that occurs after severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 
(SARS-CoV-2) infection. We present 2 cases of MIS-C after SARS-CoV-2 
vaccination; 1 patient had evidence of recent SARS-CoV-2 infection. Our findings 
suggest that vaccination modulates the pathogenesis of MIS-C.
