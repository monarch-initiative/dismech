**Authors:** Ruoff G

Rheumatoid arthritis: early treatment with corticosteroids and nonsteroidal

Ruoff G(1).

Author information:
(1)Clinical Professor of Family Medicine, Department of Family Practice, 
Michigan State University College of Medicine; Director of Clinical Research, 
Westside Family Medical Center, Kalamazoo, MI, USA.

The family physician plays several important roles in the management of patients 
with RA by early diagnosis of RA, with initiation of synthetic DMARD therapy, 
and in long-term follow-up to minimize complications of DMARD therapy and its 
impact on patient comorbidities. Three recently approved products offer some 
benefit as adjunctive therapy.
