# Interleukin-12 receptor β1 deficiency predisposing to disseminated Coccidioidomycosis.

Interleukin-12 receptor β1 deficiency predisposing to disseminated

Vinh DC(1), Schwartz B, Hsu AP, Miranda DJ, Valdez PA, Fink D, Lau KP,

Author information:
(1)Immunopathogenesis Section, Laboratory of Clinical Infectious Diseases, 
National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases, National Institutes of 
Health, Bethesda, Maryland, USA.

Severe disseminated Coccidioides is rare while localized, contained disease is 
common. We report a family with disseminated coccidioidomycosis due to a novel 
homozygous C186Y mutation in interleukin (IL)-12 receptor β1. This family 
confirms the centrality of the IL-12/IFN-γ axis to human immunity to 
Coccidioides spp.
