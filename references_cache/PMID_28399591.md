Autosomal recessive primary microcephaly (MCPH; MicroCephaly Primary Hereditary) 
is a genetically heterogeneous neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by a 
significantly reduced head circumference present already at birth and 
intellectual disability. Inconsistent features include hyperactivity, an 
expressive speech disorder, and epilepsy. Here, we provide a brief overview on 
this rare disorder pertinent for clinicians.
