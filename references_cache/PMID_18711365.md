Comment in
    Nat Genet. 2008 Sep;40(9):1042-4. doi: 10.1038/ng0908-1042.

To identify susceptibility loci for bipolar disorder, we tested 1.8 million 
variants in 4,387 cases and 6,209 controls and identified a region of strong 
association (rs10994336, P = 9.1 x 10(-9)) in ANK3 (ankyrin G). We also found 
further support for the previously reported CACNA1C (alpha 1C subunit of the 
L-type voltage-gated calcium channel; combined P = 7.0 x 10(-8), rs1006737). Our 
results suggest that ion channelopathies may be involved in the pathogenesis of 
bipolar disorder.
