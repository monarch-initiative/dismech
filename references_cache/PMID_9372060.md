Human parvovirus B19 infection.

Carper E(1), Kurtzman GJ.

Author information:
(1)Avigen, Inc., Alameda, CA 94502, USA.

Human parvovirus B19 shows remarkable tropism for human erythroid progenitor 
cells. The pathogenesis of disease resulting from infection is a dynamic between 
the viral suppression of erythropoiesis and the host's ability to mount an 
effective immune response. Understanding this process has led to strategies for 
treating chronic anemia in persistently infected patients and may result in the 
development of a vaccine to prevent infection.
