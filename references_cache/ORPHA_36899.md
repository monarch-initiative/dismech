# ORPHA:36899  Myoclonus-dystonia syndrome

**ORPHA:36899** — Myoclonus-dystonia syndrome (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Alcohol-responsive dystonia
- Hereditary essential myoclonus
- Myoclonic dystonia

## Definition

Myoclonus-dystonia syndrome (MDS) is a rare movement disorder characterized by mild to moderate dystonia along with 'lightning-like' myoclonic jerks.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant
- Not applicable

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Adult
- Age of onset: Childhood
