Neonatal Dermatology: The Normal, the Common, and the Serious.

Goodarzi H(1), Wu TT(1), Wang J(1), Teng JM(1).

Author information:
(1)Department of Dermatology, Stanford University, Palo Alto, CA.

The objective of this review is to help practitioners of neonatal and pediatric 
medicine become more familiar with diagnosing and managing neonatal skin 
conditions. This article will discuss normal neonatal skin care and benign and 
common rashes, as well as some of the serious dermatologic conditions that 
require specialists for further evaluation and/or treatment.
