We report a case of massive ascending aortic dilatation in a patient with
generalized arterial calcification of infancy (GACI). He was found to carry
compound heterozygous mutations in ABCC6 gene, previously associated with
pseudoxanthoma elasticum, although recently linked to GACI. Our case confirms
previous reports of a genotypic overlap between both entities.
