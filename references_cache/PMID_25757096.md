Hereditary orotic aciduria is a rare metabolic disease that results from a
defect of uridine-5-monophosphate synthase (UMPS). In affected patients, main
clinical symptoms are a markedly increased urinary excretion of orotic acid
combined with megaloblastic anemia. This report describes a new case of UMPS
deficiency without megaloblastic anemia but with epilepsy.
