# ORPHA:36387  Genetic epilepsy with febrile seizure plus

**ORPHA:36387** — Genetic epilepsy with febrile seizure plus (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- GEFS+
- Generalized epilepsy with febrile seizures plus

## Definition

A rare familial epilepsy syndrome characterized by febrile, focal and generalized seizures, with a spectrum of phenotypes largely variable among different family members.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Childhood
