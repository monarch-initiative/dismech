**MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_ERYTHROID** — HAY_BONE_MARROW_ERYTHROID (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of erythroid-lineage cells (red blood cell precursors and erythrocytes) from human bone marrow (Hay et al.). Erythroid cells are a non-immune myeloid lineage whose defining function is the synthesis of hemoglobin and transport of oxygen to tissues.
