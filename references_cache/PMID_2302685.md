# i(12p) in a malignant ovarian tumor.

i(12p) in a malignant ovarian tumor.

Speleman F(1), De Potter C, Dal Cin P, Mangelschots K, Ingelaere H, Laureys G, 
Benoit Y, Leroy J, Van Den Berghe H.

Author information:
(1)Department of Medical Genetics, University Hospital Gent, Belgium.

We have found one or more copies of i(12p) in an ovarian germ cell tumor, 
histologically a yolk sac tumor. This chromosome marker is characteristically 
associated with germ cell tumors in males. This report indicates that further 
investigation is necessary to establish the role of the i(12p) marker in the 
pathogenesis of germ cell tumors also in females.
