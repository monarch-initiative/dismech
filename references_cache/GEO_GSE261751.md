# Enterovirus-A71 Preferentially Infects and Replicates in Human Motor Neurons, Inducing Neurodegeneration by Ferroptosis

## Content

Enterovirus 71 (EV71) causes Hand, Foot, and Mouth Disease and has been clinically associated with neurological complications. However, there is a lack of relevant models to elucidate the neuropathology of EV71 and its mechanism, as the current models mainly utilize animal model or immortalized cell lines. In this study, we established a human motor neuron model for EV71 infection.
