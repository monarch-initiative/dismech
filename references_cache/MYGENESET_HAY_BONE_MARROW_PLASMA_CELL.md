## Description

Single-cell marker signature of plasma cells (antibody-secreting B-lineage cells) from human bone marrow (Hay et al.). Plasma cells are terminally differentiated, post-mitotic effectors of humoral immunity, characterized by massive expansion of rough endoplasmic reticulum and devoted to producing large amounts of immunoglobulin for secretion into the blood.
