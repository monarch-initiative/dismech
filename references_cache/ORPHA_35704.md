# ORPHA:35704  L-Arginine:glycine amidinotransferase deficiency

**ORPHA:35704** — L-Arginine:glycine amidinotransferase deficiency (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- AGAT deficiency

## Definition

L-Arginine:glycine amidinotransferase (AGAT) deficiency is a very rare type of creatine deficiency sydrome characterized by global developmental delay, intellectual disability, and myopathy.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Infancy
