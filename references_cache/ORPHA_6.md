# ORPHA:6  3-methylcrotonyl-CoA carboxylase deficiency

**ORPHA:6** — 3-methylcrotonyl-CoA carboxylase deficiency (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- 3-methylcrotonylglycinuria
- MCC deficiency
- MCCD

## Definition

A rare inherited disorder of leucine metabolism characterized by a highly variable clinical picture ranging from metabolic crisis in infancy to asymptomatic adults.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: All ages
