# Fatigue, arthralgia, amenorrhea--Dx?
**Authors:** Rojas-Roldan L, Wilkins T
**Journal:** J Fam Pract (2014)

## Content

1. J Fam Pract. 2014 Jun;63(6):305-8.

Fatigue, arthralgia, amenorrhea--Dx?

Rojas-Roldan L(1), Wilkins T.

Author information:
(1)Georgia Regents University, Augusta, GA, USA. Email: lrojasroldan@gru.com.

A 46-year-old Caucasian female with a history of epilepsy came into our family 
medicine center complaining of weakness, fatigue, and arthralgia that made it 
difficult for her to walk. She'd had these symptoms for 6 months and reported 
having amenorrhea and hot flashes for the past 2 years.
