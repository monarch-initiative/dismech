## Description

WikiPathways Nonalcoholic Fatty Liver Disease (NAFLD/MASLD) pathway gene set (MSigDB C2:CP:WIKIPATHWAYS, 155 genes). The set represents the major mechanistic arms of the disease: hepatic lipid accumulation via de novo lipogenesis, impaired fatty-acid beta-oxidation, and triglyceride storage in lipid droplets; insulin resistance and dysregulated insulin signalling; mitochondrial dysfunction and reactive-oxygen-species generation; progression from simple steatosis to steatohepatitis (NASH) driven by cytokine-mediated inflammation; and hepatocyte apoptosis. The WikiPathways representation captures established pathway biology and reflects the mechanistic consensus for NAFLD/MASLD at the time of curation.
