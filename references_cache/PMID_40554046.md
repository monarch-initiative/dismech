Chorea-acanthocytosis (ChAc) is a rare genetic disorder characterized by 
acanthocytosis and basal ganglia degeneration. This case series describes seven 
patients from six unrelated Tamilian families with VPS13A mutations. All 
presented with feeding dystonia, vocal tics, and dysphagia. Brain MRI showed 
caudate atrophy, and acanthocytes were detected in three patients.
