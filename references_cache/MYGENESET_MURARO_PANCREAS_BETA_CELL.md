# MYGENESET:MURARO_PANCREAS_BETA_CELL  MURARO_PANCREAS_BETA_CELL

**MYGENESET:MURARO_PANCREAS_BETA_CELL** — MURARO_PANCREAS_BETA_CELL (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of pancreatic beta cells (type B pancreatic cells) from human pancreas (Muraro et al. single-cell atlas of the human pancreas, MSigDB C8). Beta cells are the predominant endocrine cell type of the islets of Langerhans, constituting 50–80% of islet cells. Their defining function is the glucose-stimulated synthesis, packaging into dense core granules, and regulated secretion of insulin, the principal blood-glucose- lowering hormone. Impaired beta-cell function underlies all forms of diabetes mellitus.
