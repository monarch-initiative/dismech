# MYGENESET:JISON_SICKLE_CELL_DISEASE_DN  JISON_SICKLE_CELL_DISEASE_DN

**MYGENESET:JISON_SICKLE_CELL_DISEASE_DN** — JISON_SICKLE_CELL_DISEASE_DN (msigdb, C2:CGP)

## Description

MSigDB C2:CGP gene set of genes DOWN-regulated in peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMC) from patients with sickle cell disease compared to healthy subjects (Jison ML et al. 2004, PMID:15031206). The DOWN component captures the relative reduction of lymphocyte (T-cell and B-cell) signatures and ribosomal/translation programs in SCD PBMC. This relative decrease reflects the pathological inflammatory myeloid shift that accompanies SCD: the expanded and activated myeloid/inflammatory compartment dilutes the lymphoid signal, so T-cell receptor signaling, adaptive immune response, and ribosomal protein expression — hallmarks of resting lymphocytes — appear down-regulated in SCD PBMC relative to healthy controls. The set is complementary to JISON_SICKLE_CELL_DISEASE_UP, which captures the oxidant, hemolytic, and inflammatory response genes that are up-regulated in the same comparison.
