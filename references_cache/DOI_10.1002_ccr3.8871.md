# Arterial stroke in a child with Seckel syndrome with a pattern of non‐moyamoya vasculopathy
**Authors:** Samin Alavi, Mitra Khalili, Paniz Mirmoghaddam, Sharareh Kamfar, Negar Shams
**Journal:** Clinical Case Reports (2024)
**DOI:** [10.1002/ccr3.8871](https://doi.org/10.1002/ccr3.8871)

## Content

Key Clinical MessageSeckel syndrome is a rare autosomal recessive disorder, characterized by growth retardation and multiple anomalies associated with CNS vasculopathy. We describe a child with Seckel syndrome who developed a stroke due to non‐moyamoya vasculopathy.
