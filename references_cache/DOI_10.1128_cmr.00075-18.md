# Echinococcosis: Advances in the 21st Century
**Authors:** Hao Wen, Lucine Vuitton, Tuerhongjiang Tuxun, Jun Li, Dominique A. Vuitton, Wenbao Zhang, Donald P. McManus
**Journal:** Clinical Microbiology Reviews (2019)
**DOI:** [10.1128/cmr.00075-18](https://doi.org/10.1128/cmr.00075-18)

## Content

Echinococcosis is a zoonosis caused by cestodes of the genus
            Echinococcus
            (family Taeniidae). This serious and near-cosmopolitan disease continues to be a significant public health issue, with western China being the area of highest endemicity for both the cystic (CE) and alveolar (AE) forms of echinococcosis.
