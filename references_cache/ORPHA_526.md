# ORPHA:526  Liddle syndrome

**ORPHA:526** — Liddle syndrome (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Pseudoaldosteronism

## Definition

A rare genetic form of low-renin hypertension characterized by hypertension associated with decreased plasma levels of potassium and aldosterone.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Adult
- Age of onset: Childhood
- Age of onset: Infancy
