Author information:
(1)Ophthalmic Plastic Surgery, Silkiss Eye Surgery, Oakland, California, USA.
(2)Division of Ophthalmic Plastic, Reconstructive and Orbital Surgery, 
California Pacific Medical Center, San Francisco, California, USA.

We report a case of unilateral Morbihan's disease (MD) in a 55-year-old male 
presenting to a private oculofacial practice in San Francisco. Successful 
treatment with a series of triamcinolone injections to the upper and lower 
eyelids was achieved after several unsuccessful therapies. The authors provide a 
comprehensive review of the literature from 2022 to 2025 on MD.
