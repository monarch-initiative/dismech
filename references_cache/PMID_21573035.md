Osteopoikilosis is a rare asymptomatic sclerosing bony dysplasia of benign 
origin. It is usually found incidentally on radiological examinations. Familial 
occurrence indicates a genetic milieu with autosomal dominant pattern. Here, we 
present a case report of a young woman suffering from pelvic pain due to 
osteopoikilosis (OPK). The same disorder was later found in her son and 
daughter.
