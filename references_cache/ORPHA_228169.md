# ORPHA:228169  Autosomal dominant striatal neurodegeneration

**ORPHA:228169** — Autosomal dominant striatal neurodegeneration (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- ADSD

## Definition

An adult-onset movement disorder characterized by bradykinesia, dysarthria and muscle rigidity.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adult
