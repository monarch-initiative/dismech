Ehlers-Danlos syndrome.

Pearl W, Spicer M.

We have described a patient with type 4 Ehlers-Danlos syndrome who died of a 
ruptured pulmonary artery. Of the six subtypes of Ehlers-Danlos syndrome, which 
can usually be clinically differentiated, only types 1 and 4 appear to be 
associated with a substantial risk of arterial rupture.
