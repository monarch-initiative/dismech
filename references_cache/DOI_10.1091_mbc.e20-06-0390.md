# The TIM22 complex mediates the import of sideroflexins and is required for efficient mitochondrial one-carbon metabolism
**Authors:** Thomas D. Jackson, Daniella H. Hock, Kenji M. Fujihara, Catherine S. Palmer, Ann E. Frazier, Yau C. Low, Yilin Kang, Ching-Seng Ang, Nicholas J. Clemons, David R. Thorburn, David A. Stroud, Diana Stojanovski
**Journal:** Molecular Biology of the Cell (2021)
**DOI:** [10.1091/mbc.e20-06-0390](https://doi.org/10.1091/mbc.e20-06-0390)

## Content

Proteomic profiling of Sengers patient fibroblasts and AGK knockout models identifies remodeling of the mitochondrial proteome, including mitochondrial one-carbon metabolism enzymes, inner membrane serine transporters, sideroflexins, and Complex I subunits and assembly factors.
