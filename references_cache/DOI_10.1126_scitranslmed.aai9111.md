# Glucose-regulated protein 78 autoantibody associates with blood-brain barrier disruption in neuromyelitis optica
**Authors:** Fumitaka Shimizu, Kristin L. Schaller, Gregory P. Owens, Anne C. Cotleur, Debra Kellner, Yukio Takeshita, Birgit Obermeier, Thomas J. Kryzer, Yasuteru Sano, Takashi Kanda, Vanda A. Lennon, Richard M. Ransohoff, Jeffrey L. Bennett
**Journal:** Science Translational Medicine (2017)
**DOI:** [10.1126/scitranslmed.aai9111](https://doi.org/10.1126/scitranslmed.aai9111)

## Content

An autoantibody target on brain microvascular endothelial cells in patients with neuromyelitis optica may be exploited to manipulate blood-brain barrier permeability.
