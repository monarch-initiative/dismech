# ORPHA:90  Argininemia

**ORPHA:90** — Argininemia (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Arginase 1 deficiency
- Arginase deficiency
- Hyperargininemia

## Definition

A rare autosomal recessive amino acid metabolism disorder characterized by variable degrees of hyperammonemia leading to progressive loss of developmental milestones and spasticity in the absence of treatment.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Childhood
- Age of onset: Infancy
