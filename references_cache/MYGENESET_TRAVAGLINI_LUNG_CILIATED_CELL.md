# MYGENESET:TRAVAGLINI_LUNG_CILIATED_CELL  TRAVAGLINI_LUNG_CILIATED_CELL

**MYGENESET:TRAVAGLINI_LUNG_CILIATED_CELL** — TRAVAGLINI_LUNG_CILIATED_CELL (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of ciliated epithelial cells from human lung (Travaglini et al., MSigDB C8). Airway ciliated cells of the conducting airways bear hundreds of motile 9+2 cilia and are the primary drivers of mucociliary clearance, propelling mucus and trapped pathogens toward the oropharynx via coordinated ciliary beating powered by axonemal dynein motors.
