Orthopoxvirus Infections in Rodents, Nigeria, 2018-2019.

Meseko C, Adedeji A, Shittu I, Obishakin E, Nanven M, Suleiman L, Okomah D, 
Tyakaray V, Kolade D, Yinka-Ogunleye A, Muhammad S, Morgan CN, Matheny A, 
Nakazawa Y, McCollum A, Doty JB.

To investigate animal reservoirs of monkeypox virus in Nigeria, we sampled 240 
rodents during 2018-2019. Molecular (real-time PCR) and serologic (IgM) evidence 
indicated orthopoxvirus infections, but presence of monkeypox virus was not 
confirmed. These results can be used to develop public health interventions to 
reduce human infection with orthopoxviruses.
