# [A further case of Wissler's allergic subsepticemia in an adult].
**Authors:** Ratzmann KP, Finke EJ, Kraatz G, Schlenzka K, Zimmermann E
**Journal:** Z Gesamte Inn Med (1975)

## Content

1. Z Gesamte Inn Med. 1975 Jul 15;30(14):477-82.

[A further case of Wissler's allergic subsepticemia in an adult].

[Article in German]

Ratzmann KP, Finke EJ, Kraatz G, Schlenzka K, Zimmermann E.

A further case of subsepsis allergica Wissler in a 35-year-old woman is 
reported. The problems of the differential diagnosis of this rare disease are 
discussed. In the present case the disease could successfully be controlled 
using imuran in combination with corticosteroids.
