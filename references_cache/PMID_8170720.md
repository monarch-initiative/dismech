In course of experimental pre-hepatic portal hypertension in the rat, the most 
important histological alterations in the spleen, are represented by pooling of 
blood in the red pulp, activation of the macrophagic system, with hyperplasia of 
the histiocytes, increase of reticular fibers and of subcapsular 
myo-fibroblasts, and finally by evolution towards a diffuse fibrosis all over 
the parenchyma.
