**MYGENESET:HAY_BONE_MARROW_CD8_T_CELL** — HAY_BONE_MARROW_CD8_T_CELL (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of CD8-positive, alpha-beta T cells from human bone marrow (Hay et al.). CD8+ cytotoxic T cells are adaptive immune effectors that recognise peptide-MHC class I complexes on infected or transformed cells and kill them via perforin/granzyme-mediated granule exocytosis and the Fas/FasL death receptor pathway.
