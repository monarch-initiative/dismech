# ORPHA:667  Autosomal recessive malignant osteopetrosis

**ORPHA:667** — Autosomal recessive malignant osteopetrosis (Malformation syndrome, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Infantile malignant osteopetrosis

## Definition

A rare congenital disorder of bone resorption characterized by generalized skeletal densification.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Infancy
- Age of onset: Neonatal
