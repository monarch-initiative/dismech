# ORPHA:100051  Hereditary angioedema type 2

**ORPHA:100051** — Hereditary angioedema type 2 (Etiological subtype, Subtype of disorder)

## Synonyms

- HAE 2
- HAE-II
- Hereditary angioneurotic edema type 2

## Definition

Hereditary angioedema type 2 (HAE 2) is a form of hereditary angioedema characterized by acute edema in subcutaneous tissues, viscera and/or the upper airway.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: All ages
