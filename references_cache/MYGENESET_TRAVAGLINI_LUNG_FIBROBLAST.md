## Description

Single-cell marker signature of fibroblasts from human lung (Travaglini et al. lung cell atlas). Fibroblasts are stromal/mesenchymal cells that maintain the structural integrity of the lung by secreting extracellular matrix components, principally collagens and associated glycoproteins, and by orchestrating tissue remodelling and repair responses.
