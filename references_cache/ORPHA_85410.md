# ORPHA:85410  Oligoarticular juvenile idiopathic arthritis

**ORPHA:85410** — Oligoarticular juvenile idiopathic arthritis (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Oligoarticular JIA
- Pauciarticular chronic arthritis

## Definition

A rare inflammatory rheumatic disease characterized by juvenile onset arthritis that affects fewer than 5 joints during the first 6 months after disease onset.

## Inheritance

- Multigenic/multifactorial

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Childhood
