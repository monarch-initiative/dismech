Author information:
(1)Hachiya Orthopaedic Hospital, Chikusa-ku, Japan.

Two Japanese sibs with the Meckel syndrome are reported. Both babies showed the 
classical triad of this conditions: occipital encephalocele, cystic kidneys, and 
postaxial polydactyly of all four limbs. The diagnostic criteria and 
differential diagnosis were reviewed.
