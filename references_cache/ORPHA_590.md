# ORPHA:590  Congenital myasthenic syndrome

**ORPHA:590** — Congenital myasthenic syndrome (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- CMS

## Definition

Congenital myasthenic syndrome (CMS) is a group of genetic disorders of impaired neuromuscular transmission at the motor endplate characterized by fatigable muscle weakness.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant
- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Infancy
- Age of onset: Neonatal
