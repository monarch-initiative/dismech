# MYGENESET:DESCARTES_FETAL_KIDNEY_PODOCYTES  DESCARTES_FETAL_KIDNEY_PODOCYTES

**MYGENESET:DESCARTES_FETAL_KIDNEY_PODOCYTES** — DESCARTES_FETAL_KIDNEY_PODOCYTES (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of podocytes (glomerular visceral epithelial cells) from human fetal kidney (Descartes atlas, MSigDB C8). Podocytes are highly specialised, terminally differentiated epithelial cells that wrap around glomerular capillaries, extending interdigitating foot processes bridged by slit diaphragms to form the glomerular filtration barrier. The signature reflects the core structural and physiological identity of the podocyte: selective macromolecular filtration, slit- diaphragm junction biology, and foot-process cytoskeletal organisation, together with a developmental component appropriate for the fetal tissue of origin.
