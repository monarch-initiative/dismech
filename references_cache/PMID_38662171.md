The 2021 World Health Organization (WHO) classification has updated the 
definition of grade 2 gliomas and the presence of isocitrate dehydrogenase (IDH) 
mutation has been deemed the cornerstone of diagnosis. Though slow-growing and 
having a low proliferative index, grade 2 gliomas are incurable by surgery and 
complementary treatments are vital to improving prognosis. This guideline 
provides recommendations on the multidisciplinary treatment of grade 2 
astrocytomas and oligodendrogliomas based on the best evidence available.
