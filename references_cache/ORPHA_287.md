# ORPHA:287  Classical Ehlers-Danlos syndrome

**ORPHA:287** — Classical Ehlers-Danlos syndrome (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Classical EDS
- cEDS

## Definition

A rare inherited connective tissue disorder characterized by skin hyperextensibility, widened atrophic scars, and generalized joint hypermobility.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Childhood
- Age of onset: Infancy
- Age of onset: Neonatal
