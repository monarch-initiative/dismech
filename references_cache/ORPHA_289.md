# ORPHA:289  Ellis Van Creveld syndrome

**ORPHA:289** — Ellis Van Creveld syndrome (Malformation syndrome, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- Chondroectodermal dysplasia
- Mesodermic dysplasia

## Definition

A rare chondral and ectodermal dysplasia characterized by short ribs, polydactyly, growth retardation, and ectodermal and heart defects.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Antenatal
- Age of onset: Neonatal
