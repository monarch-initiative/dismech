# MYGENESET:KEGG_HYPERTROPHIC_CARDIOMYOPATHY_HCM  KEGG_HYPERTROPHIC_CARDIOMYOPATHY_HCM

**MYGENESET:KEGG_HYPERTROPHIC_CARDIOMYOPATHY_HCM** — KEGG_HYPERTROPHIC_CARDIOMYOPATHY_HCM (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy hypertrophic cardiomyopathy pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes are dominated by sarcomere and contractile apparatus proteins (beta-myosin heavy chain MYH7, myosin-binding protein C MYBPC3, actin ACTC1, troponins TNNT2/TNNI3/ TNNC1, tropomyosin TPM1, titin TTN, myosin light chains MYL2/MYL3), the dystrophin-glycoprotein complex (DMD, sarcoglycans, DAG1), and calcium-handling proteins (ATP2A2/SERCA2a, RYR2, PLN) whose mutation or dysregulation causes pathological left ventricular wall thickening and diastolic dysfunction. The set substantially overlaps the KEGG dilated cardiomyopathy gene set but emphasises the hypertrophic rather than dilated structural outcome. As a KEGG legacy pathway it reflects the established mechanistic arms and major disease genes known at the time of pathway curation.
