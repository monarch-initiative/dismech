The purpose of this overview is to: 1.. Describe the clinical characteristics of 
nonsyndromic genetic HI; 2.. Review the causes of nonsyndromic genetic HI and 
its differential diagnosis in a newborn with hyperinsulinemic hypoglycemia; 3.. 
Provide an evaluation strategy to identify the genetic cause of nonsyndromic HI 
in a proband (when possible); 4.. Inform (when possible) medical management of 
nonsyndromic genetic HI based on genetic cause and evaluation of relatives at 
risk; 5.. Inform risk assessment and surveillance of at-risk relatives for early 
detection and treatment of nonsyndromic genetic HI.
