## Description

Single-cell marker signature of alveolar epithelial type II (AT2) cells from human lung (Travaglini et al.). AT2 cells are cuboidal secretory cells that synthesize, store, and secrete pulmonary surfactant via alveolar lamellar bodies, maintain alveolar integrity, and serve as progenitors for type I alveolar epithelial cells after injury.
