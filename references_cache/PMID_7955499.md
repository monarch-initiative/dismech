Hartnup disease presenting in an adult.

Oakley A(1), Wallace J.

Author information:
(1)Waikato Hospital, Hamilton, New Zealand.

A young woman presented with pellagra. Her symptoms were precipitated by
prolonged lactation and increased activity. Dietary intake of niacin was within
recommended guidelines. Chromatography of urinary amino acids was diagnostic of
Hartnup disease, an inherited disorder usually presenting in childhood. Her
symptoms resolved with oral nicotinamide.
