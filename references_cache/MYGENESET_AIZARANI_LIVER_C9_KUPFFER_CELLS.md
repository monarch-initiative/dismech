## Description

Single-cell marker signature of Kupffer cells (resident liver macrophages) from human liver (Aizarani et al.). Kupffer cells are the primary hepatic tissue-resident macrophages that patrol the liver sinusoids, phagocytose pathogens and cellular debris, and regulate hepatic immune homeostasis via innate immune sensing, complement-mediated clearance, and cytokine secretion.
