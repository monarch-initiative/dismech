# MYGENESET:KEGG_ACUTE_MYELOID_LEUKEMIA  KEGG_ACUTE_MYELOID_LEUKEMIA

**MYGENESET:KEGG_ACUTE_MYELOID_LEUKEMIA** — KEGG_ACUTE_MYELOID_LEUKEMIA (msigdb, C2:CP:KEGG_LEGACY)

## Description

KEGG legacy acute myeloid leukemia pathway gene set (MSigDB C2 curated gene set). This is a blood cancer disease pathway gene set, not a cell-type signature. The member genes encode hematopoietic transcription factors recurrently mutated or dysregulated in AML (RUNX1, CEBPA, PML-RARA, MYC, EVI1/MECOM), growth-factor receptors and RAS/MAPK signaling components (KIT, FLT3, RAS, RAF, MAP2K, MAPK), PI3K-AKT survival effectors, and core apoptosis regulators (BCL2-family, CASP3, CYCS), reflecting the major mechanistic arms of the KEGG acute myeloid leukemia pathway: transcription-factor-driven differentiation block, growth-factor/RAS/MAPK proliferative signaling, PI3K-AKT survival signaling, and apoptosis evasion.
