# ORPHA:581  Mucopolysaccharidosis type 3

**ORPHA:581** — Mucopolysaccharidosis type 3 (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- MPS3
- MPSIII
- Mucopolysaccharidosis type III
- Sanfilippo disease
- Sanfilippo syndrome

## Definition

A group of rare lysosomal storage diseases characterized by progressive neurocognitive decline, loss of functional abilities and premature death. There are four etiological subtypes of mucopolysaccharidosis type&nbsp;3 (MPS&nbsp;III, Sanfilippo syndrome) called Sanfilippo syndrome type A, B, C, and D. Each subtype is caused by deficiency of a particular enzyme involved in the degradation of heparan sulfate leading to substrate accumulation and cellular dysfunction.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal recessive

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Childhood
