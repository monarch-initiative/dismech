# ORPHA:86875  Adult T-cell leukemia/lymphoma

**ORPHA:86875** — Adult T-cell leukemia/lymphoma (Disease, Disorder)

## Synonyms

- ATLL

## Definition

A rare, virus associated tumor due to human T-cell leukemia virus type 1 or human T-cell lymphotropic virus type 1 (HTLV-1) and is characterized by the presence of anti-HTLV-1 antibodies, and malignant, mature, medium-sized T cells with condensed chromatin and polylobated nuclei. The malignant cells exhibit a mature CD4+ T cells phenotype and express CD2, CD5, CD25, CD45RO, HLA-DR, and T-cell receptor &#945;&#946;. Presentation is heterogeneous and is typically of aggressive leukemia or lymphoma, variable skin eruptions, and visceral organ involvement.
