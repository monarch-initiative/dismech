    Nat Cell Biol. 2012 Apr 22;14(5):477-87. doi: 10.1038/ncb2490.

The attachment of stem cells to specialized functional niches instructs stem 
cell maintenance, with loss of adhesion associated with differentiation driven 
by cell-intrinsic programs. Id transcription factors are now shown to link 
cell-intrinsic maintenance programs and extrinsic cues by promoting adhesion of 
neural stem cells to the niche.
