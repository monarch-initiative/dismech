# MYGENESET:AIZARANI_LIVER_HEPATOCYTES  AIZARANI_LIVER_HEPATOCYTES

**MYGENESET:AIZARANI_LIVER_HEPATOCYTES** — AIZARANI_LIVER_HEPATOCYTES (msigdb, C8)

## Description

Single-cell marker signature of mature hepatocytes from human liver (Aizarani et al. liver atlas, MSigDB C8). Hepatocytes are the principal metabolic parenchymal cells of the liver, accounting for roughly 80% of liver volume; they execute xenobiotic detoxification via cytochrome P450 enzymes, lipid and lipoprotein metabolism, bile acid biosynthesis, synthesis of most plasma proteins including coagulation factors and complement components, and gluconeogenesis. This mature-hepatocyte signature is distinguished from the fetal hepatoblast set (DESCARTES_FETAL_LIVER_HEPATOBLASTS) by strong expression of adult-phase drug-metabolising enzymes and full hepatocyte metabolic differentiation.
