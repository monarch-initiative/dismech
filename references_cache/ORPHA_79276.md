# ORPHA:79276  Acute intermittent porphyria

**ORPHA:79276** — Acute intermittent porphyria (Disease, Disorder)

## Definition

A form of acute hepatic porphyria characterized by the occurrence of neurovisceral attacks without cutaneous symptoms.

## Inheritance

- Autosomal dominant

## Natural history

- Age of onset: Adolescent
- Age of onset: Adult
