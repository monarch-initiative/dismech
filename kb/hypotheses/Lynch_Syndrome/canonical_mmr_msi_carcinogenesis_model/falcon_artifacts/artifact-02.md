| **Endoscopically invisible dMMR crypt/gland precursor model** | Upstream qualifier to canonical adenoma-carcinoma framing | Normal-appearing colonic crypts or endometrial glands acquire biallelic MMR loss before visible neoplasia, creating occult precursor reservoirs | Walker 2023 identifies dMMR crypts/glands in normal tissue and links somatic second hits to biallelic loss; Buono 2024 and Ascrizzi 2023 discuss crypt foci/glands as earliest lesions contributing to interval cancers (pqac-00000010, pqac-00000012, pqac-00000003, pqac-00000006) | Why colonoscopy can reduce mortality yet fail to fully prevent CRC incidence in LS; why endometrial lesions may emerge from normal-appearing mucosa | Encourages liquid biopsy, stool/plasma MSI assays, enhanced imaging, and molecular surveillance in addition to conventional endoscopy |
