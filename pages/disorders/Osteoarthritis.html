Pathophysiology

3
Cartilage Degradation
Progressive loss of articular cartilage due to imbalance between catabolic and anabolic processes. Matrix metalloproteinases and aggrecanases degrade cartilage components.

Subchondral Bone Changes
Bone remodeling with subchondral sclerosis, cyst formation, and osteophyte development at joint margins. Bone changes may precede and contribute to cartilage damage.
