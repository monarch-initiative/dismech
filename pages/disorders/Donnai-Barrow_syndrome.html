Donnai-Barrow syndrome

Mendelian MONDO:0009104 Pathograph 9 hereditary disease

Donnai-Barrow syndrome is a rare LRP2-related autosomal recessive disorder characterized by multiorgan megalin dysfunction with developmental delay, sensorineural hearing impairment, ocular anomalies, and renal tubular proteinuria. Available evidence supports impaired megalin-mediated receptor-mediated endocytosis as the proximal disease mechanism.
