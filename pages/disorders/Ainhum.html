Ainhum

Complex MONDO:0007074 Dermatologic Disease Musculoskeletal Disease

Ainhum (dactylolysis spontanea) is a rare disorder characterized by progressive constricting bands of the digits, most often the fifth toe, leading to distal swelling, pain, and eventual autoamputation.

Overlapping Features Pseudo-ainhum presents with annular constriction of digits secondary to identifiable hereditary or nonhereditary conditions.
Distinguishing Features
  • Secondary to an identifiable precipitating factor or underlying disease rather than idiopathic.
