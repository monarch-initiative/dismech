Stankiewicz-Isidor syndrome

Mendelian MONDO:0054591 Pathograph 15 Neurodevelopmental disorder Genetic disease

Stankiewicz-Isidor syndrome is a PSMD12-related autosomal dominant neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by developmental delay, intellectual disability, craniofacial dysmorphism, and a variable burden of congenital malformations.
