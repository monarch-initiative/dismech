LRBA Deficiency

Mendelian MONDO:0013863 Pathograph 15 Combined immunodeficiency Primary immunodeficiency Antibody Deficiency Disorder

LRBA deficiency is a Mendelian inborn error of immunity caused by biallelic pathogenic variants in LRBA. The disorder combines humoral immune deficiency with broad immune dysregulation, including recurrent infections, hypogammaglobulinemia, autoimmunity, lymphoproliferation, and enteropathy. At a mechanistic level, LRBA loss impairs intracellular CTLA4 recycling and destabilizes immune-checkpoint control while also perturbing B-cell activation, plasmablast formation, and antibody secretion.
