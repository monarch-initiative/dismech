  nephritis. The cardinal feature is progressive renal failure, usually beginning
  in childhood and adolescence and leading to end-stage renal disease. Recent research
  highlights multiple converging mechanisms including ciliary dysfunction, disrupted
  WNT/Hedgehog/EGFR/Hippo signaling, aberrant actin-RhoA remodeling, and DNA damage
