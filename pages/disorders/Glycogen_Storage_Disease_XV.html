Glycogen Storage Disease XV

Mendelian MONDO:0013291 Pathograph 11

Glycogen storage disease XV is an autosomal recessive muscle glycogenosis caused by glycogenin-1 deficiency. The disorder produces polyglucosan body myopathy with variable proximal and distal muscle weakness, exercise intolerance, and occasional cardiac involvement.
