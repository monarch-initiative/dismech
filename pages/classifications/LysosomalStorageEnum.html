Lysosomal Storage Disease Classification

Biochemical classification of lysosomal storage diseases based on the type of accumulated substrate. Applies only to diseases caused by lysosomal enzyme deficiencies.

Schema enum: LysosomalStorageEnum Schema source: https://w3id.org/monarch-initiative/dismech/classifications/lysosomal_storage
Schema Enum Description
Classification of lysosomal storage diseases by accumulated substrate type. Values link to MONDO disease grouping terms.
  • sphingolipidosis term
    Accumulation of sphingolipids (Gaucher, Fabry, Niemann-Pick, Krabbe, etc.)
  • mucopolysaccharidosis term dismech
    Accumulation of glycosaminoglycans (MPS I through IX)
  • mucolipidosis term
    Features of both sphingolipidoses and mucopolysaccharidoses
  • glycoproteinosis term
    Accumulation of glycoproteins (fucosidosis, mannosidosis, sialidosis)
    Tagged Disorders
    • Alpha-mannosidosis
  • disorder of glycogen metabolism term
    Accumulation of glycogen in tissues (Pompe disease and related glycogen storage diseases)
    Tagged Disorders
    • Pompe Disease
  • neuronal ceroid lipofuscinosis term dismech
    Accumulation of lipofuscin in neurons (Batten disease family)
