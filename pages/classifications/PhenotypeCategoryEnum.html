Phenotype Category Classification

Broad phenotype categories corresponding to the top-level organ-system groupings in the Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO). These are the direct children of HP:0000118 (Phenotypic abnormality) and are used to classify phenotypes by affected system.

Schema enum: PhenotypeCategoryEnum Schema source: https://w3id.org/monarch-initiative/dismech/classifications/phenotype_category
Schema Enum Description
Broad phenotype categories from the Human Phenotype Ontology. Each value corresponds to a direct child of HP:0000118 (Phenotypic abnormality).
  • Abnormality of blood and blood-forming tissues Blood term
    Abnormalities of the hematopoietic system including anemias, coagulopathies, and leukocyte disorders
  • Abnormality of the breast Breast term
    Abnormalities of breast development, morphology, or function
  • Abnormality of the cardiovascular system Cardiovascular term
    Abnormalities of the heart and vasculature
  • Abnormality of the digestive system Digestive term
    Abnormalities of the gastrointestinal tract, liver, and pancreas
  • Abnormality of the ear Ear term
    Abnormalities of ear morphology or hearing
  • Abnormality of the endocrine system Endocrine term
    Abnormalities of hormone-producing glands and endocrine regulation
  • Abnormality of the eye Eye term
    Abnormalities of the eye and visual system
  • Abnormality of the genitourinary system Genitourinary term
    Abnormalities of the kidneys, urinary tract, and reproductive organs
  • Abnormality of head or neck Head and Neck term
    Abnormalities of craniofacial structures and the neck
  • Abnormality of the immune system Immune term
    Abnormalities of innate or adaptive immunity
  • Abnormality of the integument Integument term
    Abnormalities of skin, hair, nails, and sweat glands
  • Abnormality of limbs Limbs term
    Abnormalities of upper or lower limb structure
  • Abnormality of metabolism/homeostasis Metabolism term
    Abnormalities of metabolic processes and biochemical homeostasis
  • Abnormality of the musculoskeletal system Musculoskeletal term
    Abnormalities of bones, joints, muscles, and connective tissue
  • Abnormality of the nervous system Nervous System term
    Abnormalities of the central and peripheral nervous system
  • Abnormality of prenatal development or birth Prenatal and Birth term
    Abnormalities arising during prenatal development or at birth
  • Abnormality of the respiratory system Respiratory term
    Abnormalities of the airways, lungs, and respiratory function
  • Abnormality of the thoracic cavity Thoracic Cavity term
    Abnormalities of thoracic structures (pleura, mediastinum, diaphragm)
  • Abnormality of the voice Voice term
    Abnormalities of voice production and quality
  • Abnormal cellular phenotype Cellular term
    Abnormalities at the cellular level including cell morphology and behavior
  • Constitutional symptom Constitutional term
    Systemic symptoms such as fever, fatigue, and weight loss
  • Growth abnormality Growth term
    Abnormalities of growth including short stature, tall stature, and growth delay
  • Neoplasm term
    Benign or malignant neoplasms (tumors)
