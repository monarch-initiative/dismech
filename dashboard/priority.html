Scoring Guide

Broad parent means child_count >= 4 and the label is not treated as a subtype series or grouping term. That heuristic can trigger CURATE_ROOT_WITH_SUBTYPES.

Subtype series means the label matches a configured family-stem pattern and that stem appears across at least 2 candidates. If a likely parent is present, the action drops to LUMP_INTO_PARENT.

Over-specific leaf only fires when child_count == 0, the label has at least 7 words, and it contains multiple conjunctions or clause joins.

Missing from dismech and already curated are both kept in the generic scorer so mixed exports can rank curated and uncurated rows together. If one of those factors is constant or inactive in the current export, the weight controls call that out because changing it will not change the ordering.
